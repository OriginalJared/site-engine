Our Review

The Bose SoundLink Flex 2nd Gen packs Bose's signature clear, detailed audio into a rugged IP67-rated body that fits in a cargo pocket. PositionIQ technology automatically optimizes sound whether the speaker is upright, hung, or lying flat. With 12 hours of battery life, a built-in microphone for calls, and a surprisingly deep low-end for its size, it's the premium compact choice.
