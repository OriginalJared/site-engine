Our Review

The COSORI Dual Blaze solves the biggest air fryer annoyance: having to shake or flip food halfway through cooking. Dual top-and-bottom heating elements with 360° TurboBlaze technology cook food evenly from all sides without intervention. The 6.8 qt capacity is generous, the touchscreen is responsive, and 12 one-touch presets cover everything from fries to steak. It's the set-it-and-forget-it air fryer.

✅ What We Like

  • 12 presets cover nearly every food type
  • TurboBlaze is noticeably more even than single-element
