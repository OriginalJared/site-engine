Our Review

The Weber Master-Touch is the charcoal grill that's been the backyard standard for decades, refined to near-perfection. The 22" kettle provides 363 sq in of cooking space with a hinged cooking grate that lets you add charcoal without removing food. The One-Touch cleaning system sweeps ash into a removable catcher below, and the built-in thermometer monitors dome temperature. It's simple, effective, and produces that unmistakable charcoal flavor no gas grill can replicate.
