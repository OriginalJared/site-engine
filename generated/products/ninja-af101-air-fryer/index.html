Our Review

The Ninja AF101 is the entry-level Ninja air fryer and it's all most people need. The 4-quart ceramic-coated basket handles meals for 1-3 people, and four cooking functions (Air Fry, Roast, Reheat, Dehydrate) cover the essentials. Wide temperature range from 105°F to 400°F means you can dehydrate jerky or crisp up fries. It's simple, affordable, and backed by Ninja's build quality.
