Our Review

The BenQ PD2706UA is purpose-built for creative professionals, especially Mac users. Its AQCOLOR technology delivers factory-calibrated accuracy (Delta E ≤ 3) covering 99% sRGB and 95% DCI-P3. The USB-C connection with 90W Power Delivery and daisy-chain support makes it a clean one-cable solution for MacBooks. M-Book mode optimizes color rendering to match Apple's display profiles.

✅ What We Like

  • M-Book mode perfectly matches MacBook colors
  • Daisy-chain lets you connect two monitors with one cable
  • Factory calibrated and accurate
  • KVM switch for multi-device workflows

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 60Hz — no gaming utility
  • Slightly less DCI-P3 coverage than ASUS ProArt
  • Premium price for non-gaming features
