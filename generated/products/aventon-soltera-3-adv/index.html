Our Review

The Aventon Soltera 3 ADV is for riders who want exercise, not a motorcycle. The torque sensor multiplies your pedal effort rather than replacing it — ride harder, get more assist. At 42 lbs, it's light enough to ride without power and still enjoy it. The road bike geometry and 700c tires make it fast on pavement, and the hidden battery maintains a clean aesthetic. 50+ mile range handles long fitness rides. If you want an e-bike that makes you fitter rather than lazier, the Soltera is designed exactly for that purpose.
