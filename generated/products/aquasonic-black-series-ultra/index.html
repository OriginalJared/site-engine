Our Review

The Aquasonic Black Series is Amazon's best-selling electric toothbrush with 50K+ monthly purchases, and it's easy to see why. For around $40 you get an ADA-accepted sonic brush with a 40,000 VPM motor, 4 cleaning modes, wireless charging, a travel case, and 8 DuPont brush heads — enough to last over 2 years. The build quality is surprisingly good for the price, with an IPX7 waterproof rating and a sleek matte black design. It won't match a $200+ Oral-B iO or Sonicare Prestige in smart features, but for raw cleaning power per dollar, nothing else comes close.

✅ What We Like

  • Unbeatable value — 8 heads + case + wireless charging for $40
  • ADA accepted — not just marketing hype
  • 50K+ bought per month on Amazon — proven track record
  • 4-week battery life is excellent

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • No pressure sensor
  • Build quality doesn't match premium brands long-term
  • Sonic intensity may feel aggressive to sensitive gums at first
  • No app or smart features

Key Features

  • 40,000 vibrations per minute — powerful sonic motor
  • ADA Accepted for plaque removal and gingivitis prevention
  • 8 DuPont brush heads included (~2.5 years of use)
  • Wireless charging with 4-week battery life
  • 4 modes: Clean, Soft, Whiten, Massage
