Our Review

The ThinkPad X1 Carbon Gen 12 continues the legacy of the world's most respected business laptop. Intel Core Ultra processors bring AI acceleration, the 14" 2.8K OLED display option is stunning, and the legendary ThinkPad keyboard remains the best in the industry. At just 2.48 lbs with up to 15 hours of battery, it's built for professionals who live on their laptop. MIL-STD-810H durability means it survives the road warrior lifestyle.
