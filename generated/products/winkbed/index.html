Our Review

The WinkBed is designed with back pain sufferers in mind. Its exclusive Lumbar Layer — a gel-infused foam pad positioned directly under the lower back — provides extra support exactly where most sleepers need it. The Euro pillow top is hand-tufted for durability, and the 3-zone pocketed coil system keeps your spine aligned regardless of sleep position. Four firmness options from Softer to Plus (for heavier sleepers) cover every body type. Handmade in the USA with a 120-night trial.
