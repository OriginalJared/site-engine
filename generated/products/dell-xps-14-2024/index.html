Our Review

The Dell XPS 14 pushes design boundaries with a near-borderless InfinityEdge display that extends to the very edges of the chassis. Intel Core Ultra processors handle demanding workloads, and the 14.5" 3.2K OLED touchscreen makes content creation a joy. The zero-lattice keyboard and seamless glass trackpad are visually striking, though they take some adjustment. For users who want the most futuristic-looking laptop available, this is it.

✅ What We Like

  • Jaw-dropping edge-to-edge display
  • OLED touchscreen is vibrant and accurate
  • Premium build quality throughout
  • Thunderbolt 4 connectivity

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Zero-lattice keyboard is polarizing
  • Function row is a capacitive touch bar
  • Webcam is only 1080p
  • Fan noise under load
