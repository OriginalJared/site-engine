Our Review

Ring is the most recognized name in home security, and the Outdoor Cam Plus is their best all-around camera. 2K video provides clear detail for identifying faces and license plates. The battery-powered design means no wiring — mount it anywhere with the included tools. Ring's app is the most polished security camera app available, with easy live viewing, event timeline, and sharing. Alexa integration lets you view cameras on Echo Show devices with a voice command. Ring Protect subscription ($4/month) unlocks video history and person detection.
