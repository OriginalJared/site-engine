Our Review

The Anker Soundcore Motion 300 delivers features you'd expect at twice the price. It's one of the few budget speakers with Hi-Res Audio Wireless certification (LDAC codec support), and its SmartTune technology auto-adjusts EQ based on speaker orientation. The 13-hour battery, IPX7 waterproofing, and customizable EQ via the Soundcore app make it a ridiculously good deal at $79.

✅ What We Like

  • LDAC support at this price is unheard of
  • SmartTune auto-EQ works well
  • Excellent app with deep EQ controls
  • Great value at $79

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • IPX7 not IP67 — no dust rating
  • 13 hours is average
  • Build feels plasticky compared to JBL/Bose
