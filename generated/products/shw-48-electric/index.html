Our Review

The SHW 48-inch is the desk you buy when the budget is tight and you just want to stand. At $160, it's the lowest-priced electric standing desk in this roundup, and you feel it in the 110 lb capacity and single-motor lift. But for a laptop and a single monitor, it works. The height range of 27 to 46 inches covers most users, and the desk comes with a basic hook for headphones and a small shelf for peripherals. The 1-year warranty is short, but at this price, the barrier to entry is almost zero.

✅ What We Like

  • Unbeatable price for an electric sit-stand desk
  • Good enough for laptop + single monitor
  • Very easy and fast assembly
  • Lightweight — easy to move between rooms

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 110 lb capacity — not for heavy setups
  • 1-year warranty is the shortest here
  • Single motor is slow and audible
  • Noticeable wobble at max height with heavy loads

Key Features

  • Lowest price electric standing desk in the roundup
  • 48" x 24" desktop with headphone hook
  • Single-motor electric lift
  • Small peripheral shelf included
  • Simple 15-minute assembly
