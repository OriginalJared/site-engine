Our Review

The COSORI Pro LE L501 is the best-selling air fryer on Amazon for good reason. It delivers consistent, crispy results with minimal oil across 9 one-touch cooking presets. The 5-quart basket handles meals for 2-4 people, the shake reminder buzzes mid-cook to prompt flipping, and the non-stick basket is dishwasher safe. At under $100, it's the easiest entry point into air frying.
