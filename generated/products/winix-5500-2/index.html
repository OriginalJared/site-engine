Our Review

The Winix 5500-2 pairs a True HEPA filter with Winix's proprietary PlasmaWave technology, which creates hydroxyls that break down pollutants at the molecular level — something passive HEPA filters can't do. This makes it especially effective against odors, VOCs, and airborne pathogens. The washable AOC (Advanced Odor Control) carbon filter is a cost-saver — just rinse it instead of replacing it. Smart sensors automatically adjust the fan speed, and the sleep mode drops noise to a barely audible 27.8 dB. Consistently rated among the top 3 air purifiers by every major review outlet.
