Our Review

The Google Nest Wifi Pro integrates seamlessly into Google's smart home ecosystem. Each node covers up to 2,200 sq ft with Wi-Fi 6E tri-band performance, and the 3-pack blankets up to 6,600 sq ft. Matter and Thread support make it a smart home hub, and Google Home app management is intuitive. The minimalist design blends into any room, and automatic software updates keep security current without any effort.
