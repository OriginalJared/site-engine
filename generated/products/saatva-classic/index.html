Our Review

The Saatva Classic is the most awarded online mattress for good reason. Its dual-coil system combines individually wrapped comfort coils with a durable support base for responsive, buoyant support that memory foam can't match. A Euro pillow top adds plush surface comfort, and the organic cotton cover is breathable and soft. Three firmness options and two height options mean you can dial in exactly the feel you want. Free white-glove delivery and mattress removal seal the deal.

✅ What We Like

  • Responsive innerspring feel — no 'stuck in' sensation
  • Three firmness levels cover every preference
  • White-glove delivery and removal is rare
  • 365-night trial with free returns

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $1,395 is mid-premium pricing
  • Heavier than foam mattresses — hard to move
  • Edge support could be stronger on Plush Soft
  • Not compressed — can't easily transport yourself
