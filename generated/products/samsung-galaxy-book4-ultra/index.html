Our Review

The Samsung Galaxy Book4 Ultra is Samsung's answer to the MacBook Pro. It pairs an Intel Core Ultra 9 185H with an NVIDIA RTX 4070 Laptop GPU for workstation-class performance in a sleek chassis. The 16" 3K AMOLED display with 120Hz is vivid and smooth, and Galaxy AI features integrate with Samsung phones for a connected ecosystem. At 3.4 lbs for a 16" machine with a discrete GPU, the weight-to-power ratio is impressive.

✅ What We Like

  • RTX 4070 in an ultrabook form factor
  • AMOLED display is stunning
  • 32GB RAM standard is generous
  • Great Samsung ecosystem integration

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $2,399 is very expensive
  • Battery life drops fast under GPU load
  • Samsung bloatware out of the box
  • Speakers are average for the price
