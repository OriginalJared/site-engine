Our Review

The Camp Chef Woodwind Wi-Fi 24 solves the biggest pellet grill weakness: searing. Its patented Slide and Grill technology opens a direct-flame zone so you can sear steaks over real fire — something most pellet grills can't do. Wi-Fi connectivity with the Camp Chef app lets you monitor cooks remotely, and PID temperature control maintains ±5°F accuracy. The Ash Kickin cleanout system makes post-cook cleanup a breeze. At $799, it undercuts Traeger while adding sear capability.
