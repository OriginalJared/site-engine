Our Review

The Razor E Prime III is built for the last-mile problem — getting from the bus stop or train station to your office. At just 22 lbs and foldable, it's the lightest adult electric scooter that's actually usable. The 250W hub motor reaches 18 mph, and the tubeless pneumatic front tire provides some cushion. The anti-rattle folding mechanism stays tight over time, and the rear fender brake provides simple, reliable stopping. It's not the fastest or longest-range, but it's the easiest to carry.

✅ What We Like

  • 22 lbs is lightest in class — easy to carry
  • Foldable design is compact for transit
  • 18 mph is decent for last-mile use
  • Anti-rattle mechanism stays tight

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 15-mile range is short
  • 250W motor struggles on hills
  • Rear solid tire transmits bumps
  • Fender brake is less effective than disc
