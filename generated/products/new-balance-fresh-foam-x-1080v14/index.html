Our Review

The New Balance 1080v14 is the ultimate comfort shoe for runners who want their daily miles to feel like running on clouds. The Fresh Foam X midsole is New Balance's softest and most responsive foam, and the Hypoknit upper wraps your foot in a seamless, sock-like fit. The strategic rubber outsole provides traction where you need it while keeping weight down. If your priority is comfort on runs of any distance, the 1080v14 is the answer.

✅ What We Like

  • Plushest ride of any daily trainer
  • Hypoknit upper fits like a sock
  • Available in wide and extra-wide
  • Excellent for long runs and recovery

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $160 is at the top of the price range
  • Soft ride may feel unstable for some
  • Not designed for speed work
  • Outsole coverage could be more extensive
