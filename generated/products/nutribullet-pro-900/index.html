Our Review

The NutriBullet Pro 900 is the personal blender that started the extraction revolution. Blend directly in the 32oz or 24oz travel cups, twist on a lid, and take your smoothie out the door. 900 watts of power handles frozen fruit, seeds, and leafy greens with ease. The simple twist-and-blend operation means there are no buttons to press — just load, twist, and go. It's the fastest path from ingredients to smoothie, every single morning.
