Our Review

The Netgear Orbi RBKE963 is the most powerful mesh system money can buy. Its quad-band architecture dedicates an entire 6GHz band to wireless backhaul, leaving three bands free for devices. A 10 Gbps Ethernet port on the router handles multi-gig internet plans, and the 3-pack covers up to 9,000 sq ft. If you have a large home, gigabit+ internet, and demand the absolute fastest Wi-Fi everywhere, this is it — but you'll pay dearly for it.

✅ What We Like

  • Quad-band with dedicated backhaul is the fastest architecture
  • 10 Gbps port for multi-gig internet
  • 9,000 sq ft coverage handles any home
  • Wired backhaul option for maximum reliability

  • $1,499 is extremely expensive
