Our Review

The Glorious Model O 2 Wireless refines the original Model O formula with a solid shell (no holes), the BAMF 2.0 sensor, and Glorious' improved wireless technology. At 68g with a low-profile ambidextrous shape, it's excellent for fingertip and claw grip players. The aggressive $79 price undercuts most competitors while delivering comparable performance and a premium feel.

✅ What We Like

  • Excellent value at $79 for wireless
  • Solid shell feels premium
  • Low-profile shape great for fingertip grip
  • Good battery life for the weight

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Side buttons are a bit mushy
  • Scroll wheel steps feel light
  • Less refined than Logitech/Razer flagships

Key Features

  • BAMF 2.0 sensor — 26K DPI
  • 68g solid shell (no holes)
  • 2.4GHz wireless + Bluetooth
  • 80-hour battery life
  • Low-profile ambidextrous shape
  • Glorious CORE software
