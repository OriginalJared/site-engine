Our Review

The Bissell ICONpet Turbo Edge focuses on what most vacuums neglect: edges and corners. Its unique EdgeReach foot design allows the brush roller to extend to the very edge of the vacuum head, cleaning right up against walls and baseboards. The tangle-free brush roll handles pet hair, 30 minutes of fade-free suction provides consistent power, and every purchase helps fund Bissell's pet rescue foundation. It's the vacuum for detail-oriented cleaners.
