Our Review

The BenQ TK700STi is purpose-built for gamers who want a massive screen without the massive price of a 100"+ TV. True 4K resolution at 16ms input lag at 1080p/240Hz means responsive gameplay on a screen up to 150". The short-throw lens projects a 100" image from just 6.5 feet away, and 3,000 lumens keeps the image vibrant even with lights on. Built-in Android TV and dedicated Game Mode HDR round out the ultimate gaming projector.

✅ What We Like

  • 16ms input lag is competitive for gaming
  • Short-throw is great for small rooms
  • True 4K — not pixel-shifted
  • 3,000 lumens handles ambient light well

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $1,299 is premium pricing
  • DLP technology can produce rainbow effect
  • Fan noise is noticeable during gaming sessions
  • HDR performance is limited
