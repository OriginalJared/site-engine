Our Review

The Unagi Model One Voyager is the scooter for people who care about aesthetics as much as performance. Its magnesium alloy deck, carbon fiber handlebar stem, and Japanese-designed silhouette make it the best-looking scooter on the market. Dual 250W motors (front + rear) provide all-wheel traction and hill-climbing ability, and the 500Wh battery delivers up to 25 miles of range. At 26 lbs, it's one of the lightest dual-motor scooters available.
