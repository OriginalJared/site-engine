Our Review

The Rove R2-4K Dual Pro delivers flagship video quality at a budget price. The Sony STARVIS 2 sensor captures sharp 4K footage day and night, and the included 128GB microSD card means zero extra costs out of the box. 5GHz Wi-Fi transfers footage to your phone quickly, and built-in GPS logs your speed and route. The 3-inch IPS display lets you review footage without a phone. Loop recording and G-sensor auto-lock ensure important footage is never overwritten. Amazon's #1 best-selling dash cam with 10K+ monthly sales. MotorTrend's 'Best Night Performance' pick.
