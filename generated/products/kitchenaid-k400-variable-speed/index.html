Our Review

The KitchenAid K400 brings the brand's iconic design aesthetic to the countertop blender. Its asymmetric blade creates a vortex that pulls ingredients down for smoother results without a tamper, and the soft-start feature ramps up gradually to prevent splashing. The 56oz BPA-free pitcher, 5-speed control with pulse, and self-cleaning cycle make it practical, while the die-cast metal base and 10+ color options make it a kitchen centerpiece.

✅ What We Like

  • Gorgeous design in many colors
  • Asymmetric blade blends without a tamper
  • Soft-start prevents splashing
  • Solid die-cast metal build quality

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $199 for a non-Vitamix blender
  • Not as powerful as Vitamix or Ninja flagships
  • 56oz pitcher is mid-range capacity
  • Variable speed isn't as precise as Vitamix
