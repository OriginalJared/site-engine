Our Review

The Marshall Emberton II looks like a miniature guitar amp and sounds like one too. Its True Stereophonic 360-degree sound fills rooms with warm, rich audio that has a distinctly musical character. The 30-hour battery life is simply outstanding — nearly double most competitors. IP67 waterproofing, a multi-directional control knob, and the iconic Marshall aesthetic make it a statement piece.
