Our Review

The Samsung Odyssey G7 28" brings 4K gaming to life with a 144Hz refresh rate and HDMI 2.1 for next-gen consoles. The IPS panel delivers wide viewing angles and vibrant colors, while 1ms response time keeps motion blur at bay. G-Sync and FreeSync Premium Pro compatibility ensures tear-free gameplay. It's the sweet spot for gamers who want both 4K detail and high refresh rate without breaking the bank.

✅ What We Like

  • 144Hz at 4K is buttery smooth
  • HDMI 2.1 works perfectly with consoles
  • Great color accuracy for an IPS gaming panel
  • Reasonable price for 4K 144Hz

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • HDR400 is entry-level HDR — not impressive
  • No USB-C connectivity
  • Stand lacks height adjustment on base model

Key Features
