Our Review

The LG CordZero A9 Kompressor solves the small-bin problem that plagues cordless vacuums. Its unique Kompressor technology physically compresses dust and debris inside the bin, fitting 2.4x more before you need to empty it. Two swappable batteries deliver up to 120 minutes of total runtime, and the Aqua Spinner mop attachment adds wet cleaning capability. Power Drive Nozzle automatically adjusts suction for carpet and hard floors.

✅ What We Like

  • Kompressor means far fewer bin empties
  • Two batteries give outstanding runtime
  • Mop attachment adds versatility
  • Lightweight at 5.5 lbs

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Suction is good but not class-leading
  • Kompressor mechanism adds complexity
  • Mop attachment is basic compared to Tineco
  • Brand is less recognized in vacuums
