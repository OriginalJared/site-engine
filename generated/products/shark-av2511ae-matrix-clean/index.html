Our Review

Shark's Matrix Clean system uses a precision grid pattern to ensure the robot covers every inch of floor space, eliminating the random missed patches common with cheaper robots. The self-emptying base holds 60 days of debris, and the bagless design saves on replacement costs. LiDAR navigation maps your home accurately, and the app supports room-specific cleaning and scheduling. At $350, it's positioned between budget and premium — you get reliable American brand support and solid performance without the bleeding-edge features (or prices) of the flagship Chinese robots.
