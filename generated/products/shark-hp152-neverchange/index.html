Our Review

The Shark HP152's killer feature is right in the name: NeverChange. The HEPA filter is rated for 5 years of use before replacement — compared to 6-12 months for every competitor. Over 5 years, that saves $150-400 in filter costs. The Clean Sense IQ technology automatically adjusts fan speed based on air quality, and the purifier covers up to 650 sq ft. It captures 99.98% of particles down to 0.1 microns (smaller than standard HEPA's 0.3 micron rating). At $200 with virtually zero ongoing costs for 5 years, the total cost of ownership is lower than a $50 purifier with $30 semi-annual filter replacements.
