Our Review

The Breville Barista Express has been the most recommended home espresso machine for years, and it still earns that title. The built-in conical burr grinder means you're grinding fresh for every shot — the single biggest factor in espresso quality. PID temperature control ensures consistent extraction, and the 15-bar Italian pump delivers proper pressure. The dose-control grinding and manual tamping give you full control over your shots. The steam wand produces microfoam for latte art. CNET, Forbes, and Food Network all name it their top pick. At $650, it replaces $5 daily cafe visits in about 4 months.
