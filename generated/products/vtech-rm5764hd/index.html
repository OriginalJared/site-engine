VTech RM5764HD Smart Baby Monitor

Our Review

The VTech RM5764HD offers smart monitor features at a budget-friendly price. It streams 1080p video to both a dedicated 5" parent unit AND your phone via the V-Sight app — giving you the flexibility of both worlds. Remote pan, tilt, and 2x zoom cover the room, and the night light with lullabies helps soothe your baby back to sleep. At $119, it undercuts most smart monitors while matching their core features.

✅ What We Like

  • Dedicated screen AND phone app — best of both
  • 1080p at $119 is outstanding value
  • Night light and lullabies are handy extras
  • VTech reliability and brand trust

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • App can be sluggish
  • Wi-Fi connection needed for app features
