Our Review

The Breville Super Q matches Vitamix performance while solving the biggest blender complaint: noise. Its Noise Suppression technology makes it measurably quieter than any competitor at full power. The 2 HP motor with 12-speed control and 5 one-touch programs (Green Smoothie, Frozen Dessert, Soup, Crushed Ice, Auto Clean) deliver Vitamix-level results. The 68oz Tritan pitcher, LCD timer, and auto-purée feature round out a blender that's genuinely a pleasure to use.

✅ What We Like

  • Noticeably quieter than Vitamix and Ninja
  • 2 HP motor matches Vitamix power
  • LCD timer and auto-purée are smart features
  • 68oz pitcher handles large batches

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $499 is nearly Vitamix territory
  • Less brand cachet than Vitamix
  • Noise suppression adds bulk to the base
  • Pitcher is Tritan plastic, not glass
