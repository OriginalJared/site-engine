Our Review

The Wolfbox G900 TriPro is unlike any other dash cam — it replaces your rearview mirror entirely with a 12-inch touchscreen displaying live camera feeds. Three cameras cover front (4K), rear mirror view (1080p), and a bumper-mounted rear camera (1080p) for the widest coverage available. The mirror display eliminates rear blind spots by showing a wider field than any physical mirror. Touch the screen to switch views, pinch to zoom on footage, or swipe through recordings. MotorTrend called it a 'standout favorite' and staff members have used it for years to eliminate blind spots.
