Our Review

The Framework Laptop 16 is a revolution in laptop design. Every component is user-replaceable: RAM, SSD, battery, keyboard, trackpad, screen, and even the GPU via a dedicated expansion bay. Choose your own port configuration with swappable expansion cards, and upgrade your GPU years later without buying a new laptop. It's the anti-disposable laptop, built for people who want to buy once and upgrade forever.

✅ What We Like

  • Most repairable and upgradeable laptop in existence

  • Swap ports to match your needs
  • Strong community and open-source ethos

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Battery life is average (8-10 hours)
