Our Review

The Samsung Jet 75 Pet is built specifically for pet households. Its turbo action brush with anti-tangle technology powers through pet hair on carpets, upholstery, and hard floors without wrapping around the roller. 200W of suction and a long-lasting removable battery deliver up to 60 minutes of cleaning. The lightweight 6 lb body and included pet multi-tool and mini motorized brush make reaching every fur-covered corner easy.

✅ What We Like

  • Anti-tangle brush is a must for pet owners
  • 60-minute runtime handles whole homes
  • Removable battery for easy swapping
  • Lightweight at 6 lbs
