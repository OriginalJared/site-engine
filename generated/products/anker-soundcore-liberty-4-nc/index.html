Anker Soundcore Liberty 4 NC

The cheapest good ANC earbuds — under $60 with adaptive noise cancellation and 10-hour battery.

Our Review

At $57, the Soundcore Liberty 4 NC are the most affordable noise-canceling earbuds worth recommending. Adaptive ANC adjusts to your environment in real-time, and the 98.5% noise reduction claim holds up well against commuter noise and office chatter. Sound quality is warm and bass-forward — great for pop and hip-hop, less ideal for classical. 10 hours of battery per charge is excellent, and the case adds another 40 hours. The Soundcore app offers a full EQ, ANC customization, and even a HearID test that personalizes sound to your hearing profile. NBC Select's 'Best Budget' pick.
