Our Review

The G502 X Plus is the wireless evolution of Logitech's most popular gaming mouse ever. It packs the HERO 25K sensor, LIGHTFORCE hybrid switches, a reversible DPI-shift button, and a free-spinning scroll wheel into a sculpted ergonomic body. Full RGB LIGHTSYNC and up to 130 hours of battery life round out a mouse that does everything well.

✅ What We Like

  • Incredible customization and button count
  • Dual-mode scroll wheel is addictive
  • 130-hour battery life
  • Premium build and materials

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Heavy at 106g compared to modern ultralights
  • Complex shape isn't for everyone
  • Expensive for a non-ultralight mouse

Key Features
