Our Review

TP-Link's Tapo line has quickly become the go-to for budget security cameras, and the C420 is their best outdoor model. 2K QHD resolution with a starlight sensor captures usable footage even in near-darkness without spotlights. The rechargeable battery lasts 180 days, and the magnetic mount installs in seconds. All footage stores locally on a microSD card — no subscription needed. The Tapo app is clean and responsive with live view, playback, activity zones, and smart detection. At $100 with zero ongoing costs, it undercuts Ring and Blink while matching their core features.
