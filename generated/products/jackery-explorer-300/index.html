Our Review

The Jackery Explorer 300 is the power station for people who value portability above all else. At 7.1 lbs, it's lighter than most laptops and small enough to fit in a daypack. 293Wh of capacity and 300W output handle phones, laptops, cameras, drones, and LED lights with ease. It won't power heavy appliances, but that's not its job — it's the ultimate device charger for camping, road trips, and emergency phone charging. Amazon's #1 best-selling portable power station with 7,000+ units sold monthly.
