Our Review

The Garmin Venu 3 bridges the gap between fitness tracker and smartwatch better than anything else. The AMOLED display is vibrant and always-on, battery life reaches up to 14 days in smartwatch mode (vs 18 hours for Apple Watch), and Garmin's training features are unmatched — Training Readiness, Body Battery, HRV status, and nap detection. It also supports phone calls via Bluetooth speaker and mic. Garmin Connect is the most comprehensive fitness app available, with decades of data science behind its algorithms. If fitness tracking is your primary reason for wearing a smartwatch, nothing else comes close.
