Our Review

The Nespresso Vertuo Next uses centrifusion technology — spinning each capsule at up to 7,000 RPM to extract coffee with a rich, crema-topped finish. The barcode reader on each Vertuo capsule automatically adjusts brewing parameters for that specific coffee. Five cup sizes from espresso to full 18oz carafe mean one machine covers every coffee need. The slim, eco-friendly design (made with 54% recycled plastic) fits small kitchens beautifully.
