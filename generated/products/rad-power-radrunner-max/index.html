Our Review

The RadRunner Max is the SUV of electric bikes. Its long rear rack carries a passenger (with optional passenger kit), cargo, or kid seats. The 750W motor and 48V battery provide 45+ miles of range, and the step-through frame makes mounting easy even with a loaded bike. Fat tires absorb bumps, and the front suspension fork smooths out rough roads. Rad Power is the largest e-bike brand in North America with dedicated customer support centers. For families, delivery workers, and anyone who needs to haul stuff, the RadRunner Max replaces car trips.
