Our Review

The Apollo City Pro bridges the gap between commuter scooters and performance machines. Its 500W sustained (1000W peak) motor pushes to 28 mph, and the 748Wh battery provides up to 38 miles of range. Dual spring suspension front and rear absorbs city potholes, and the triple braking system (regenerative + dual disc) provides confident stopping power. IP66 water resistance handles any weather, and the foldable design makes it practical for mixed commutes.
