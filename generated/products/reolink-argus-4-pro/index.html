Our Review

The Reolink Argus 4 Pro combines a 180-degree ultra-wide view with 4K resolution from dual lenses — it covers the area of two regular cameras in one. The included solar panel keeps it perpetually charged, and the 8GB onboard storage plus free Reolink Cloud (1GB) means no subscription is needed. Color night vision with spotlights, two-way audio, and smart person/vehicle detection complete the package. The Reolink app is straightforward and reliable. For users who want maximum coverage with minimum cameras and zero ongoing costs, this is the pick.
