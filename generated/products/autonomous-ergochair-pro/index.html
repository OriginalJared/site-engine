Our Review

Autonomous' ErgoChair Pro offers 11 points of adjustment — more than any other chair under $500. Seat height, depth, tilt, tilt tension, tilt range, back recline, back height, lumbar support, headrest height, headrest tilt, and armrest position are all independently adjustable. The woven mesh back provides excellent airflow, and the molded foam seat stays comfortable through 8-hour days. At $449 direct from Autonomous, it undercuts comparable chairs from Branch and Steelcase. The 2-year warranty is shorter than competitors, but the adjustability range is unmatched at this price.
