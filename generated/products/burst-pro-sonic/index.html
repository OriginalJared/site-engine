Our Review

The BURST Pro takes everything good about the Original and levels it up. The headline feature is the 9-month battery life — charge it once and forget about it for most of the year. A smart pressure sensor (missing on the Original) now provides real-time feedback to protect your gums. The LED display shows your current mode, and 5 brushing modes (Sensitive, Clean, Whitening, Massage, Freshen) cover every scenario. The ultra-soft tapered bristles remain BURST's calling card, reaching below the gumline without irritation. At $90, it slots between budget picks and premium flagships — offering the key features of a $200 brush at half the price.
