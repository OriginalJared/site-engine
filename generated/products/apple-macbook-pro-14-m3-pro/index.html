Our Review

The MacBook Pro 14" M3 Pro is the tool professionals reach for when performance matters. The M3 Pro chip with 12-core CPU and 18-core GPU tears through video editing, 3D rendering, and software development. The Liquid Retina XDR display with 1600 nits peak HDR brightness is the best screen on any laptop. 17 hours of battery life, 18GB unified memory, and a full suite of ports (HDMI, SD card, MagSafe, Thunderbolt 4) make it the complete package for power users.
