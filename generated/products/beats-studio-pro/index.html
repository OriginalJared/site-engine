Our Review

Beats Studio Pro solve a common problem: most premium headphones favor either Apple or Android. The Studio Pro work equally well with both. iPhone users get Apple's spatial audio and seamless pairing. Android users get enhanced codec support and the Beats app. USB-C lossless audio (when wired) delivers studio-quality sound regardless of platform. ANC is effective, the sound is balanced with Beats' signature punch, and 40 hours of battery handles a work week. At $250 (frequently on sale for less), they're the best cross-platform premium headphones.

✅ What We Like

  • True cross-platform — equal features on iOS and Android
  • USB-C lossless audio is excellent
  • 40-hour battery is generous
  • Frequently on sale under $200

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • ANC trails Sony and Bose
  • Sound can be bass-heavy for some genres
  • On-head detection is occasionally unreliable
  • Beats brand perception as 'bass-only' is outdated but persists

Key Features

  • Full features on both iPhone AND Android
  • USB-C lossless audio when wired
  • Personalized Spatial Audio (Apple) + enhanced Android codec
  • 40-hour battery with ANC
  • Foldable with premium carrying case
