Our Review

The FelixKing is the most popular budget office chair on Amazon for a reason. At $130, you get an adjustable headrest, a removable lumbar pillow, breathable mesh back, flip-up armrests, and a smooth tilt mechanism. It's not going to win awards for build quality or long-term durability, but for a home office, dorm, or secondary workstation where you need a comfortable chair without a big investment, it delivers more ergonomic features than any other chair at this price. Assembly takes about 20 minutes, and the 250 lb weight capacity covers most users.

✅ What We Like

  • Most features per dollar under $150
  • Flip-up armrests are genuinely useful
  • Breathable mesh keeps you cool
  • Quick and easy 20-minute assembly

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 250 lb capacity is limiting
  • Lumbar pillow attaches with velcro — can slip
  • Seat cushion compresses after 1-2 years
  • 1-year warranty is short

Key Features
