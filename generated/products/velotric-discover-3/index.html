Our Review

The Velotric Discover 3 is designed for comfort above all else. The ultra-low step-through frame lets riders mount without lifting their leg high — ideal for seniors and anyone with mobility limitations. The upright riding position reduces back and neck strain, and the wide comfort saddle supports longer rides. The torque sensor provides smooth, natural assist, and the 750W motor handles hills without drama. 80+ mile range is the longest in this roundup. Integrated lights, fenders, and a rear rack make it practical for errands and commuting. If comfort is your priority, this is the bike.
