Our Review

The Keurig K-Supreme Plus SMART brings intelligence to pod brewing. Its BrewID technology scans each K-Cup and automatically adjusts temperature, brew size, and strength to the manufacturer's recommended settings — ensuring every pod brews at its best. MultiStream Technology uses 5 entry points instead of one for more even extraction. Wi-Fi connectivity enables firmware updates and usage tracking via the Keurig app. If you're committed to K-Cups, this is the best way to brew them.

✅ What We Like

  • BrewID optimizes every pod automatically
  • MultiStream extraction noticeably improves flavor
  • 78 oz reservoir means less refilling
  • Over Ice setting brews stronger for iced coffee

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • K-Cups are expensive vs. ground coffee
  • Pod waste is an environmental concern
  • Wi-Fi features feel gimmicky
  • Can't brew a full carafe
