Our Review

The Apple Watch Series 10 remains the smartwatch to beat for iPhone owners. The always-on Retina display is brighter and thinner than ever, and the S9 chip enables on-device Siri and double-tap gesture control. Health features include FDA-cleared ECG, blood oxygen monitoring, skin temperature sensing, and sleep apnea detection. Crash detection and fall detection can automatically call emergency services. The depth gauge and water temperature sensor make it swim-friendly. With watchOS 11, the Apple Watch is less a notification device and more a genuine health companion.
