Our Review

The Core 400S is LEVOIT's allergy-focused flagship. Its medical-grade H13 HEPA filter captures 99.97% of particles at 0.3 microns, and the laser dust sensor provides precise PM2.5 readings displayed on the unit and in the app. Auto mode uses these readings to adjust fan speed in real-time — it noticeably ramps up when you cook or vacuum, then quietly settles back down. The VeSync app offers scheduling, air quality history, and voice control via Alexa/Google. At $190, it's the most feature-complete purifier under $200 for allergy sufferers.

✅ What We Like

  • Laser dust sensor is more accurate than LED-based sensors
  • App shows air quality trends over time
  • Auto mode is responsive and effective
  • Strong brand with proven reliability

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Coverage area (403 sq ft) is smaller than the Vital 200S
  • Carbon filter is basic for odors
  • Laser sensor can be sensitive to steam/humidity
  • Night light can't be fully disabled

Key Features
