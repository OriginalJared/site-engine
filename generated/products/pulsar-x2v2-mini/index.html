Our Review

The Pulsar X2V2 Mini is a favorite among competitive FPS players who prefer claw grip. At just 52g with the PixArt PAW3395 sensor, it offers raw precision in a clean symmetrical shell with zero holes. The shape is carefully contoured for aggressive claw and fingertip control, and the build quality punches well above its price. Dual wireless modes and 70 hours of battery round it out.

✅ What We Like

  • 52g with a solid shell — no honeycomb holes
  • Perfect shape for claw grip players
  • PAW3395 sensor is flawless
  • Clean, minimal design

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Too small for large hands or palm grip
  • Side buttons could be clickier
  • 70-hour battery is decent but not class-leading

Key Features

  • PixArt PAW3395 sensor
  • 52g ultra-lightweight
  • Symmetrical shape (no holes)
  • Dual wireless (2.4GHz + BT)
  • 70-hour battery life
  • Encoder-based scroll wheel
