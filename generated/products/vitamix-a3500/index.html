Our Review

The Vitamix Ascent A3500 is the flagship of the Vitamix lineup. It combines the brand's legendary 2.2 HP motor with a digital touchscreen timer, 5 program presets (Smoothie, Hot Soup, Frozen Dessert, Dips, Self-Clean), and wireless container recognition that auto-adjusts blending for different container sizes. The 64oz low-profile container fits under cabinets, and the 10-year warranty is the best in the industry. It's the blender for people who want Vitamix perfection with modern convenience.
