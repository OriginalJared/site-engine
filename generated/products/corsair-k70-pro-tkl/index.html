Our Review

The Corsair K70 PRO TKL is built for competitive gaming. Its 8000Hz polling rate (8x faster than standard) means your keystrokes register in 0.125ms — imperceptible to humans but measurably faster in competitive scenarios. The TKL (tenkeyless) layout gives you more desk space for mouse movement, and the detachable USB-C cable makes it tournament-portable. Per-key RGB backlighting is fully customizable through iCUE software, and the PBT double-shot keycaps won't develop shine. The aluminum top plate adds rigidity and premium feel. Corsair has been a trusted name in competitive peripherals for over a decade.

✅ What We Like

  • Fastest polling rate for competitive advantage
  • PBT keycaps won't develop shine
  • Detachable cable for portability
  • Corsair iCUE is powerful and mature
