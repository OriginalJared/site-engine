The Galaxy Watch 7 is Samsung's mainstream flagship and the best smartwatch for most Android users. The 3-in-1 BioActive sensor handles heart rate, ECG, and body composition analysis in one chip. Wear OS 5 with Samsung's One UI Watch overlay delivers smooth performance and access to the full Google Play Store. The sapphire crystal display is tough enough for daily wear, and the aluminum case keeps weight down. Samsung Health provides comprehensive fitness tracking with auto-workout detection, sleep coaching, and stress management. At $300, it undercuts the Apple Watch while matching most of its health features.
