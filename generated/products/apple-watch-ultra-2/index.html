Our Review

The Apple Watch Ultra 2 is built for people who push limits. The 49mm titanium case survives drops, impacts, and extreme temperatures that would destroy a regular Apple Watch. Dual-frequency GPS provides the most accurate location tracking Apple offers — critical for trail running, open-water swimming, and backcountry hiking. The 36-hour battery life (72 hours in low power mode) finally addresses Apple Watch's biggest weakness. The Action Button provides one-touch access to workouts, waypoints, or shortcuts. The 2000-nit display is readable in direct sunlight. If you need an Apple Watch that can survive anything, this is it.
