Our Review

The Roborock Qrevo CurvX brings a curved rubber side brush that reaches into corners and along baseboards better than any flat-edge competitor. With 8000Pa suction, dual spinning mop pads, and a dock that empties dust, washes mops with hot water, and refills the robot's tank automatically, this is about as close to truly hands-free floor care as it gets. LiDAR navigation maps your home in a single run, and the app lets you set no-go zones, schedule room-specific cleanings, and adjust suction per surface. At around $800, it undercuts the premium flagships while matching most of their performance.
