Our Review

The Lenovo IdeaPad Flex 5i hits the sweet spot between price and versatility. Its 14" Full HD touchscreen with 360-degree hinge works as a laptop, tablet, or tent, and the Intel Core i5-1335U handles everyday tasks and light creative work with ease. 16GB RAM and a 512GB SSD provide plenty of room, and the included digital pen adds note-taking and sketching capabilities. It's the affordable 2-in-1 that doesn't feel cheap.

✅ What We Like

  • Excellent value for a 2-in-1 with pen
  • 16GB RAM and 512GB SSD at $549
  • Solid build quality for the price
  • Good keyboard for a convertible

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Display is FHD — no OLED at this price
  • Battery is average at 10 hours
  • Integrated graphics limit creative work
  • Pen doesn't store inside the laptop
