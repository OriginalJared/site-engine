The De'Longhi Rivelia does everything automatically. Drop in beans, press a button, and it grinds, tamps, extracts, and froths milk for 18 different drink recipes — from espresso to flat white to cold brew. The LatteCrema system produces cafe-quality microfoam automatically, and the color touchscreen makes drink selection intuitive. The bean hopper is removable and sealed to keep beans fresh, and the entire brew group is removable for easy cleaning. Forbes' 'Best Fully Automatic' pick.
