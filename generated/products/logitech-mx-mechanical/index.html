Our Review

Logitech's MX Mechanical brings mechanical key feel to their beloved MX productivity line. The low-profile tactile switches provide satisfying feedback without the height and noise of traditional mechanical keyboards. Logi Options+ software is the best keyboard customization app available — per-app key remapping, macros, and Logitech Flow lets you move your cursor seamlessly between multiple computers. The keyboard works with Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android, with printed legends for both Mac and Windows. Bluetooth connects to 3 devices with easy switching. The smart backlight activates when your hands approach.
