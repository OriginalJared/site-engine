Our Review

The Hiboy S2 Pro is perfect for riders who never want to deal with flat tires. Its 10" solid honeycomb tires are puncture-proof and maintenance-free, while the 350W motor delivers 19 mph and the 36V battery provides up to 25 miles of range. The rear dual suspension absorbs bumps that solid tires would otherwise transmit, and the foldable design with app connectivity makes it a practical daily commuter. At $459, it's outstanding value for a solid-tire scooter.

✅ What We Like

  • Puncture-proof tires = zero tire maintenance
  • Rear suspension compensates for solid tires
  • 25-mile range at this price is competitive
  • Foldable and relatively light at 36 lbs

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Solid tires transmit more vibration than pneumatic
  • 19 mph is slower than competitors
  • 350W struggles on steep hills
  • Build quality is budget-tier
