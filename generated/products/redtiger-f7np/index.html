Our Review

The REDTIGER F7NP competes directly with the Rove R2-4K at the same $110 price point. The Sony STARVIS 2 sensor delivers sharp 4K footage with excellent night performance, and the included rear camera provides 1080p coverage behind your vehicle. 5.8GHz Wi-Fi transfers large files fast, and built-in GPS logs your routes and speed. The free SD card in the box means zero additional costs. WDR (Wide Dynamic Range) handles harsh lighting transitions like tunnel exits. With 6K+ monthly sales on Amazon, it's proven its reliability at scale.
