Our Review

The CASABREWS CM5418 is Amazon's #1 best-selling espresso machine, and at $122 it's easy to see why. The 20-bar pump (more than enough for proper extraction), stainless steel construction, and functional steam wand deliver real espresso at a fraction of premium prices. The 34oz removable water tank is easy to fill, and the drip tray catches spills cleanly. It won't match a Breville in temperature stability or grind precision, but for someone who wants to make real espresso (not pod coffee) without spending $500+, this is the starting point. CNET's 'Best Budget' pick.
