Our Review

The Nebula Capsule 3 Laser is a full-featured laser projector that fits in your hand. At just 1.9 lbs and roughly the size of a soda can, it packs 300 ANSI lumens of laser brightness, Google TV with all major streaming apps, and a built-in speaker. The laser light source means instant on/off with no warm-up, and 2.5 hours of battery life makes it truly portable. Auto-keystone and auto-focus mean zero setup fuss. It's the ultimate travel projector.

✅ What We Like

  • Incredibly portable — fits in a bag pocket
  • Laser light source is bright and instant
  • Google TV for seamless streaming
  • 2.5-hour battery for movie night anywhere

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 300 lumens needs a dark room
  • Only 1080p resolution
  • Small built-in speaker is tinny
  • Not a home theater replacement
