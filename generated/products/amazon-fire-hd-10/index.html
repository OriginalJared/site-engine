Our Review

The Amazon Fire HD 10 is the tablet you buy for the couch, the kitchen, and the kids. At $140, it delivers a 10.1-inch 1080p display, 10+ hours of battery, and deep Alexa integration — ask it to control your smart home, play music, or check the weather hands-free. It runs Amazon's Fire OS with access to Netflix, Disney+, YouTube, Kindle, and most streaming apps. It won't win any performance awards, but for media consumption, web browsing, and casual games, it does exactly what most people need a tablet to do. The microSD slot adds up to 1TB of storage.

✅ What We Like

  • Best value tablet for streaming
  • Alexa integration is genuinely useful
  • microSD expansion up to 1TB
  • Great for kids with parental controls

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Fire OS — no Google Play Store
  • Performance is sluggish for multitasking
  • Camera quality is poor
  • Ads on lock screen (pay $15 to remove)

Key Features

  • 10.1" 1080p display
  • 10+ hour battery life
  • Alexa hands-free built-in
  • microSD slot up to 1TB
  • Show Mode turns it into an Echo Show
