Our Review

The Garmin X310 combines best-in-class 4K video quality with Garmin's signature driver-assist features. The built-in Clarity Polarizer reduces windshield glare without needing a separate accessory — a feature unique to Garmin. Forward collision and lane departure warnings add a layer of safety, and the Garmin Drive app makes footage transfer easy via Wi-Fi. The 140-degree field of view captures the full road ahead, and the included 32GB microSD card means it works out of the box. MotorTrend's 'Best Overall' pick after hands-on testing of 9 dash cams.
