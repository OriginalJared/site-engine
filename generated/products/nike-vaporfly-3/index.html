Our Review

The Nike Vaporfly 3 is the shoe that changed marathon running forever. Its full-length carbon fiber plate sits between layers of ZoomX foam — Nike's lightest, most responsive material — to create a propulsive sensation that makes you faster. Studies show it improves running economy by 4-5%. It's the shoe worn by the majority of elite marathoners and the holder of countless world records. If you're racing, this is the unfair advantage.

✅ What We Like

  • Scientifically proven to make you faster
  • ZoomX + carbon plate = unmatched energy return
  • Ultralight at 6.6 oz
  • The gold standard for race day

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $260 is expensive for a race-only shoe
  • Not durable enough for daily training

  • Narrow fit — not for wide feet
