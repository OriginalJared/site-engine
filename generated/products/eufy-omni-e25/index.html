Our Review

The eufy Omni E25 punches way above its $400 price tag. It packs 20,000Pa suction (matching robots twice its price), a HydroJet roller mop that scrubs rather than just drags, and an all-in-one dock that empties dust, washes the roller, and dries it with hot air. The zero-tangle roller brush is a genuine innovation for pet owners — hair wraps around a blade and gets cut automatically. AI obstacle avoidance keeps it from eating cables and socks. For $400, this is arguably the best value in robot vacuums right now.
