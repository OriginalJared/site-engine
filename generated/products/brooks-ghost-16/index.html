Our Review

The Brooks Ghost 16 is the go-to shoe for runners who want a plush, smooth ride. The nitrogen-infused DNA LOFT v2 midsole is 20% softer and 10% more responsive than the previous version, creating a cushioned experience that protects joints without feeling mushy. The 3D Fit Print upper wraps your foot securely, and the segmented crash pad in the outsole ensures smooth heel-to-toe transitions. It's the most comfortable daily trainer money can buy.
