Our Review

The Ninja AF150AMZ XL steps up from the AF101 with a 5.5-quart capacity that comfortably feeds 3-5 people. It keeps the same reliable Ninja performance with 5 cooking functions (adding Bake to the lineup) and a wide 105°F–400°F temperature range. The XL crisper plate ensures airflow around food for maximum crispiness, and the dishwasher-safe parts make cleanup painless. It's the Goldilocks air fryer.

✅ What We Like

  • Ninja reliability at a reasonable price
  • Dishwasher-safe everything
