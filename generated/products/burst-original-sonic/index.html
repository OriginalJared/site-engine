Our Review

BURST built its brand through dental professional recommendations, and the Original Sonic is their core product. At 33,000 sonic vibrations per minute, it removes up to 10x more plaque than a manual brush. The standout feature is the ultra-soft, interdental-cut PBT nylon bristles — they're noticeably gentler than competitors while still delivering deep cleaning. BURST claims (and clinically backs) a 9x reduction in gum bleeding within 2 weeks. Three modes (Whitening, Sensitive, Massage), a long-lasting rechargeable battery, and a charcoal-infused brush head option round out a compelling mid-range package.
