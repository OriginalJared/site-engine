Our Review

The Ninja Foodi DualZone changes the game with two independent 4-quart baskets that can cook different foods at different temperatures and times, finishing together with the Smart Finish feature. DualZone technology means you can air fry chicken in one basket and roast veggies in the other — no juggling, no waiting. Six cooking functions, dishwasher-safe baskets, and a 8-quart total capacity make it the most versatile air fryer available.

✅ What We Like

  • Two baskets cooking simultaneously saves massive time
  • Smart Finish ensures both foods are done together
  • 8 qt total capacity feeds a family of 4-6
  • Dishwasher-safe parts make cleanup easy

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Large footprint takes up counter space

Key Features
