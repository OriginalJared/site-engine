The Hbada E3 hits the sweet spot between budget and premium. At $230, you get a built-in adjustable lumbar support system (not a removable pillow), 2D armrests, an adjustable headrest, and a modern curved design that looks professional. The mesh back and seat provide full breathability, and the 300 lb capacity handles most users comfortably. The tilt mechanism is smooth with adjustable tension and a lock position. It's the chair you buy when the $130 options feel too cheap and the $400+ options feel too expensive. 3-year warranty and straightforward assembly.
