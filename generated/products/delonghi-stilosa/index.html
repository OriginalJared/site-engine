Our Review

The De'Longhi Stilosa is the absolute entry point for real espresso. At $100, you get a 15-bar pump, a manual steam wand, and De'Longhi's Italian engineering heritage. It uses pre-ground coffee or ESE pods, so no grinder is needed. The learning curve is steeper than a Nespresso, but the reward is genuine espresso you made yourself. The steam wand produces passable foam for lattes and cappuccinos. It's small, simple, and gets the job done. For anyone who wants to learn espresso without a big investment, the Stilosa is where to start.
