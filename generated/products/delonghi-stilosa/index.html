The De'Longhi Stilosa is the absolute entry point for real espresso. At $100, you get a 15-bar pump, a manual steam wand, and De'Longhi's Italian engineering heritage. It uses pre-ground coffee or ESE pods, so no grinder is needed. The learning curve is steeper than a Nespresso, but the reward is genuine espresso you made yourself. For anyone who wants to learn espresso without a big investment, the Stilosa is where to start.
