Our Review

The Eureka RapidClean Pro is proof that a good cordless vacuum doesn't need to cost $500. At just $129 and 5.2 lbs, it's light enough for anyone to use comfortably. LED headlights illuminate dust, the high-efficiency brush roll handles both hard floors and low-pile carpet, and up to 40 minutes of runtime covers small to mid-size homes. It's not fancy, but for daily quick cleanups or as a secondary vacuum, the value is unmatched.
