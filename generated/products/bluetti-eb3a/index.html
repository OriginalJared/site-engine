The BLUETTI EB3A is the entry point to LiFePO4 portable power. At $199, it packs 268Wh of capacity, 600W continuous output, and charges from 0-80% in 30 minutes via wall outlet. It won't run a fridge, but it handles laptops, phones, CPAP machines, drones, and small appliances easily. The built-in wireless charging pad on top is a nice touch. At just 10.1 lbs, it's genuinely portable — throw it in a backpack for camping or keep it in a closet for emergencies. Outdoor Life's 'Best Budget' pick.
