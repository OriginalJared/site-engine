Our Review

The Fire 7 Kids is the tablet parents buy without worrying. The kid-proof case survives drops, spills, and the general chaos of childhood. Amazon's 2-year worry-free guarantee means if your kid breaks it, Amazon replaces it — no questions asked. Amazon Kids+ provides age-appropriate content, games, and videos with robust parental controls. Parents can set time limits, bedtime schedules, and educational goals. The 7-inch size is perfect for small hands. At $100, it's cheap enough that you don't stress about it. The best first tablet for kids ages 3-7.
