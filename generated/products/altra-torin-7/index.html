Our Review

The Altra Torin 7 is for runners who believe in letting their feet move naturally. Its zero-drop platform places your heel and forefoot at the same height, encouraging a natural midfoot strike. The foot-shaped FootShape toe box lets your toes spread fully for better balance and power. Altra EGO midsole foam provides moderate cushioning without interfering with ground feel. If you want to run the way your body was designed to, the Torin 7 is the gateway.

✅ What We Like

  • Zero-drop promotes natural running form
  • FootShape toe box is incredibly comfortable
  • Encourages midfoot strike and proper mechanics
  • Great for runners with wide forefeet

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Zero-drop requires transition period
  • Not for runners who need heel support
  • Less cushion than max-cushion competitors
  • Looks unconventional due to wide toe box
