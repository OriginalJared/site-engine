Our Review

The UPLIFT V2 Commercial is a workhorse built for people who demand the best. It pairs a 60-inch desktop with a dual-motor frame rated for 355 lbs, meaning even the heaviest multi-monitor battlestation won't faze it. The height range spans 22.6 to 48.7 inches, and the advanced keypad lets you save four presets for quick transitions. UPLIFT backs it with a 7-year warranty and one of the best customer-service reputations in the industry. If your budget allows it, this is the desk you keep for years.
