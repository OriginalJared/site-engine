Our Review

The Weber Go-Anywhere is the grill you throw in the car for camping, tailgating, or beach trips. Its rectangular design provides 160 sq in of cooking space — enough for 6 burgers — and the foldable legs lock the lid shut for easy transport. Porcelain-enameled steel resists rust and retains heat, and the adjustable dampers give you temperature control. At $59, it's the cheapest Weber and the best portable charcoal grill by a wide margin.
