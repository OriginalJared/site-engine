Our Review

The Dreame R20 punches above its weight with 150 AW of suction — matching vacuums twice its price. The anti-tangle brush roller uses a V-shaped design that flings hair off instead of wrapping, and the HD color display shows battery, mode, and maintenance info. Up to 90 minutes of runtime on a single charge handles even large homes, and the included accessories cover everything from crevices to mattresses.

✅ What We Like

  • 150 AW suction at $329 is outstanding value
  • Anti-tangle brush actually works
  • 90-minute runtime is among the best
  • HD display is informative and clear

⚠️ Worth Noting
