Our Review

The Ninja Detect Duo Power Blender Pro gives you two blenders in one base. It auto-detects whether you've attached the 72oz pitcher or the 24oz single-serve cup and adjusts power accordingly via BlendSense technology. The 1800W motor is one of the most powerful in the consumer market, and 6 preset programs handle everything from smoothies to soup to frozen drinks. It's the best of both worlds — family batches and personal blends from one machine.
