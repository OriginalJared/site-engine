Our Review

The Coway AP-1512HH has been the most recommended air purifier by independent reviewers for half a decade, and the reason is consistency. Its 4-stage filtration (pre-filter, deodorization filter, True HEPA, vital ionizer) handles particles, odors, and VOCs. The eco mode automatically shuts off the fan when air quality is good for 30 minutes, saving energy. The air quality indicator ring changes color in real-time so you can see the purifier working. At $160, it's slightly more than budget picks but the track record of reliability and performance justifies every penny.
