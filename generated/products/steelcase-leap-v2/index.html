Our Review

The Steelcase Leap V2 is the chair physical therapists recommend. Its LiveBack technology allows the backrest to flex and adapt to your spine's natural movement as you shift positions throughout the day — no manual adjustment needed. The lower back firmness control lets you dial in exactly how much lumbar pressure you want. Unlike mesh chairs, the Leap uses a high-density foam seat that many find more comfortable for 8+ hour days. The 12-year warranty matches Herman Miller, and Steelcase's build quality is legendary. If you have back problems, this is the chair to try first.
