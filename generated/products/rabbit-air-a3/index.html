Our Review

The Rabbit Air A3 is the air purifier you buy when you want the absolute best. Its 6-layer filtration system includes a customizable filter that you choose based on your specific concern — germ defense, toxin absorber, odor remover, or pet allergy. The BioGS HEPA filter is designed to maintain efficiency over time rather than degrading like standard HEPA filters. At 20.3 dB on its lowest setting, it's the quietest purifier in this roundup. It covers up to 1,070 sq ft, mounts on the wall to save floor space, and can even be customized with art panels. Forbes' #1 pick for 2026.
