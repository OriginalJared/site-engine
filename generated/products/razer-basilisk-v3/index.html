Our Review

The Razer Basilisk V3 delivers a premium wired experience at a mid-range price. Its Focus Pro 26K sensor, HyperScroll tilt wheel with smart-shift, and 11 programmable buttons make it one of the most versatile wired mice available. Razer Chroma RGB adds flair, while the ergonomic thumb rest keeps long sessions comfortable.

✅ What We Like

  • Smart scroll wheel is a game-changer
  • Excellent sensor at this price
  • Comfortable ergonomic shape
  • Tons of programmable buttons

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Wired only — no wireless option
  • 101g is heavy by modern standards
  • Scroll wheel can be noisy in ratchet mode

Key Features

  • Focus Pro 26K sensor
  • HyperScroll tilt wheel with smart-shift
  • 11 programmable buttons
  • Razer Chroma RGB (13 zones)
  • Ergonomic thumb rest
  • 100% PTFE feet
