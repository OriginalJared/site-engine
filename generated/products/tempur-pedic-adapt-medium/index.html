Our Review

Tempur-Pedic invented memory foam mattresses, and the TEMPUR-Adapt Medium is their most accessible model. The proprietary TEMPUR material responds to your body's temperature, weight, and shape for personalized support that improves over time. The SmartClimate cover wicks moisture and feels cool to the touch, and the all-foam construction eliminates motion transfer almost completely. If you want the deepest, most body-conforming pressure relief available, nothing beats TEMPUR.
