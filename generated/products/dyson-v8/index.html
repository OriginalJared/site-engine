Our Review

The Dyson V8 is the entry point into Dyson's cordless lineup and it's all most people need. Whole-machine HEPA filtration captures 99.99% of particles, the direct-drive cleaner head digs deep into carpet, and 40 minutes of runtime handles apartments and small homes. At $349, it delivers the Dyson engineering and build quality without the flagship price. It's the smart Dyson for budget-conscious buyers.
