Not everyone wants a chunky smartwatch on their wrist. The Fitbit Charge 6 is for people who want health and fitness tracking in a slim, comfortable band they can wear 24/7 without noticing. The 7-day battery means weekly charging, not daily. The built-in GPS tracks outdoor runs and walks without your phone.
