Our Review

The Garmin Dash Cam Live is the first dash cam with built-in LTE connectivity. This means you can check your car's camera feed from anywhere via the Garmin Drive app — at work, on vacation, whenever you want to see what's happening around your vehicle. Cloud storage saves footage automatically without SD card management. Live view, event-triggered alerts, and remote parking monitoring make it the most connected dash cam available. 1440p recording, GPS, and Garmin's driver assists (collision/lane warnings) complete the package. The LTE subscription ($7/month after trial) is the ongoing cost. PCMag's Editors' Choice.
