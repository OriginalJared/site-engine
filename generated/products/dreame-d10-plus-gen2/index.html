Our Review

The Dreame D10 Plus Gen 2 is proof that the robot vacuum floor has risen dramatically. For $200 you get LiDAR navigation, a self-emptying base, and 6000Pa suction — features that cost $400+ just two years ago. It won't win any awards for mopping (it has a basic water tank and drag pad), but as a vacuum-first robot for daily floor maintenance, it's hard to beat at this price. The app supports scheduling, no-go zones, and room-specific settings. If you've never owned a robot vacuum and want to try one without committing $500+, this is where to start.

✅ What We Like

  • Absurdly cheap for LiDAR + self-emptying
  • Navigation is accurate and efficient
  • Good daily maintenance vacuum
  • Dreame app is solid
