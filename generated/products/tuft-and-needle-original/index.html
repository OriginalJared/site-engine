Our Review

The Tuft & Needle Original proved that a great mattress doesn't need to cost $1,000. Their proprietary T&N Adaptive foam is infused with graphite and cooling gel for heat dissipation, and the open-cell structure allows airflow that solid memory foam blocks. It's firmer than most foam mattresses (7/10), making it ideal for back and stomach sleepers who need support. At $595 for a queen, it's the best no-frills mattress from a brand you can trust.

✅ What We Like

  • Under $600 for a quality foam mattress
  • Graphite + gel cooling works well
  • Firmer feel great for back/stomach sleepers
  • Simple design — nothing to break down

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Too firm for most side sleepers
  • Only one firmness option
  • All-foam — no coil support
