Our Review

The Segway Ninebot Max G2 is the electric scooter that does everything well. Its 43-mile max range is the longest in the commuter class, powered by a 551Wh battery and a 450W motor that hits 22 mph. Built-in turn signals, a front suspension fork, and Apple Find My tracking add safety and peace of mind. The 10" pneumatic tires absorb bumps, and the IPX5 water resistance handles rain. It's the scooter that turns a commute into a joy ride.

✅ What We Like

  • 43-mile range eliminates range anxiety
  • Built-in turn signals are a huge safety upgrade
  • Apple Find My for theft tracking
  • Tank-like build quality
