Our Review

Jackery is the Apple of portable power — everything just works without reading a manual. The Explorer 1000 v2 upgrades to LiFePO4 chemistry with 4,000+ cycle life, charges to full in just 1 hour with emergency mode via the app, and weighs only 23.8 lbs with a foldable handle. Three AC outlets, USB-C PD 100W, and a 12V car port cover every charging scenario. The interface is dead simple — a clear LCD shows input/output watts and remaining time. Jackery's #1 best-seller on Amazon for good reason.
