Our Review

If you live in the Samsung ecosystem, the Galaxy Buds 4 Pro are the obvious choice. They auto-switch between your Galaxy phone, tablet, and laptop, and the Galaxy Wearable app offers deep customization including a 9-band EQ, 360 Audio head tracking, and ANC level adjustments. Call quality is exceptional — the 3-mic system with AI noise reduction makes you sound clear even in noisy environments. ANC performance has caught up to Bose and Sony, and the blade-style design sits flush in your ear. SSC Hi-Fi codec delivers studio-quality streaming from Samsung devices.
