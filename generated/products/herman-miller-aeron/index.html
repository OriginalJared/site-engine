Our Review

The Herman Miller Aeron has been the benchmark for ergonomic seating for over 25 years, and the remastered version improves on a legend. The all-mesh 8Z Pellicle suspension distributes weight evenly while allowing airflow — no more sweaty back during long sessions. PostureFit SL supports both the lumbar and sacral regions of your spine, and every adjustment (arms, tilt, height, forward lean) is intuitive. Available in three sizes (A, B, C) to fit different body types. The 12-year warranty covers everything. It's expensive, but people keep these chairs for a decade or more.

✅ What We Like

  • 12-year warranty — best in the industry
  • All-mesh construction prevents overheating
  • PostureFit SL is the best lumbar system tested
  • Holds resale value extremely well
