Our Review

The 9900 Prestige is Philips' answer to the question 'what if a toothbrush was actually smart?' SenseIQ technology monitors your brushing pressure, motion, and coverage 100 times per second, automatically adjusting intensity to protect your gums while maximizing cleaning. The A3 Premium All-in-One brush head delivers up to 20x more plaque removal than a manual brush. With 5 modes, 3 intensity levels (15 total settings), a premium charging travel case, and seamless app integration, this is the Rolls-Royce of sonic toothbrushes. At $380, it's an investment — but for oral health enthusiasts, it's the pinnacle.
