Our Review

The Soundcore Life Q30 have been a budget ANC staple for years, and they remain one of the best values in headphones. Hybrid ANC with three modes (Transport, Indoor, Outdoor) adapts to your environment. The Soundcore app offers a full parametric EQ so you can tune the sound exactly to your preference — a feature missing from most budget headphones. 50 hours of battery in ANC mode, 60 hours without, means you charge them twice a month at most. Business Insider's 'Best Budget' pick. At $56, they're the headphones to beat under $100.
