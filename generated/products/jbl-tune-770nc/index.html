Our Review

JBL's Tune 770NC pack a staggering 70 hours of battery life into a sub-$100 package. Adaptive noise cancellation adjusts to your environment, and Smart Ambient mode lets you control how much outside sound you hear. The JBL sound signature is bass-forward and energetic — great for workout playlists and pop music. The foldable design and lightweight build make them travel-friendly. The JBL Headphones app offers a full EQ, ANC customization, and a 'find my headphones' feature. At $80, they compete with headphones twice their price on battery life alone.

✅ What We Like

  • 70-hour battery is unmatched at any price
  • Adaptive ANC works well for the price
  • JBL's fun, bass-forward sound
  • Foldable and lightweight for travel
