Our Review

The Purple Original Mattress uses a proprietary GelFlex Grid — a hyper-elastic polymer grid that flexes under pressure points (shoulders, hips) while staying firm under your back. The result is a unique feel that's neither memory foam nor innerspring — it's bouncy, supportive, and naturally temperature-neutral because air flows through the grid's open cells. If you've tried foam and springs and loved neither, Purple's grid technology might be your answer.

✅ What We Like

  • Unique feel unlike any other mattress
  • Naturally cool — no gel or phase-change needed
  • Excellent for combination sleepers (easy to reposition)
  • No off-gassing issues

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • GelFlex Grid feel is polarizing — love or hate
  • Lighter sleepers may not compress the grid enough
  • Edge support is weak
  • Heavier than comparable foam mattresses
