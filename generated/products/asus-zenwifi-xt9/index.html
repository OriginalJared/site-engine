Our Review

The ASUS ZenWiFi XT9 is built for users who want both ease of use and deep control. Tri-band AX7800 speeds with a dedicated 5GHz backhaul keep traffic flowing, while ASUS AiProtection Pro (powered by Trend Micro) provides lifetime free security without subscriptions. AiMesh compatibility means you can mix and match with other ASUS routers, and the ASUS Router app offers granular controls that rival full router firmware.

✅ What We Like

  • Deep customization via ASUS Router app
