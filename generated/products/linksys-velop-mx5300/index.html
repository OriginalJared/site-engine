Our Review

The Linksys Atlas Max 6E brings enterprise Qualcomm networking silicon to the home. Each node packs a quad-core Qualcomm processor and tri-band Wi-Fi 6E (AXE8400), with Cognitive Mesh technology that uses machine learning to optimize channel selection, band steering, and client routing. The 3-pack covers up to 9,000 sq ft, and a 5G Ethernet port handles ultra-fast internet plans. It's the choice for tech-savvy users who want maximum throughput.

✅ What We Like

  • Qualcomm chipset delivers top-tier performance
  • 9,000 sq ft coverage handles very large homes
  • 5G Ethernet for multi-gig internet
  • Cognitive Mesh learns and optimizes over time

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $699 is expensive for most households
  • Nodes are large
  • Linksys app is less polished than eero or Deco
