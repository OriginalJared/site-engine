Our Review

The LG CineBeam HU710PB uses a hybrid laser/LED light source for exceptional color accuracy and a 30,000-hour light source life — that's over 20 years of typical use. The 4K UHD resolution with HDR10 and HLG produces detailed, vibrant images, and LG's webOS smart platform provides direct access to Netflix, Disney+, and other apps without an external device. Lens shift, 1.6x zoom, and a quiet 30dB operation in Eco mode make it a refined home theater choice.

✅ What We Like

  • 30,000-hour light source eliminates lamp replacements
  • webOS is the best built-in smart TV platform
  • 1.6x zoom + lens shift for flexible placement
  • 30dB in Eco mode is whisper-quiet
