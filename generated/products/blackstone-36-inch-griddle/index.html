Our Review

The Blackstone 36" Griddle isn't a traditional grill — it's a flat-top griddle that opens up an entirely different world of outdoor cooking. Smash burgers, eggs, pancakes, stir-fry, fajitas, fried rice — anything you'd cook on a restaurant flat-top, you can cook here. Four independently controlled burners provide 60,000 BTU across a massive 720 sq in cooking surface. The cold-rolled steel top develops a natural non-stick seasoning over time, getting better with every cook.

✅ What We Like

  • 720 sq in is the largest cooking surface available
  • Flat-top opens up cooking options grills can't do
  • Seasoning builds natural non-stick over time
  • 4 heat zones for different temperatures

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Not a traditional grill — no grill marks
  • Requires seasoning and maintenance
  • Heavy and large — needs dedicated space
  • No lid — can't do indirect/convection cooking

Key Features

  • 720 sq in cold-rolled steel cooking surface
  • 4 independent burners (60,000 BTU)
  • Rear grease management system
  • Paper towel holder + side shelves
  • Foldable legs for transport
  • Electronic ignition
