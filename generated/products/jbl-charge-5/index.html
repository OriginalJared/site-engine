Our Review

The JBL Charge 5 remains the portable speaker to beat. It delivers rich, room-filling JBL Pro Sound with a dedicated tweeter and dual bass radiators, wrapped in an IP67 waterproof and dustproof body. The built-in power bank charges your devices via USB-A, and the 20-hour battery means all-day listening. PartyBoost lets you link multiple JBL speakers for bigger sound.
