Our Review

If you want the look of an executive leather chair with basic ergonomic features, the Amazon Basics Ergonomic Executive is the safest budget pick. The bonded leather upholstery looks professional, the flip-up armrests save space, and the adjustable lumbar support provides basic lower back relief. At $170, it won't match the ergonomic performance of a mesh chair, but for an office where appearance matters and you're not sitting 10+ hours daily, it does the job. Amazon's return policy makes it risk-free to try, and the padded seat is comfortable for moderate use.
