The Ride1Up Roadster V3 is for riders who want an e-bike that looks and feels like a regular bicycle. At just 33 lbs, it's lighter than most non-electric bikes. The belt drive is silent and maintenance-free, and the internal hub motor is invisible — no one will know it's electric unless you tell them. The slim battery hides inside the downtube. 500W motor provides enough boost for city riding and moderate hills, with 30-60 miles of range depending on assist level. If you want the e-bike experience without the e-bike look, the Roadster V3 is the only choice.
