Our Review

The Dreame X50 Ultra is the first robot vacuum with retractable ProLeap legs that physically climb over door tracks and raised thresholds up to 2.36 inches — a game-changer for multi-room homes. Pair that with 20,000Pa suction, a 220-minute battery, 80-degree hot water mopping built into the robot itself, and AI obstacle avoidance that recognizes 100+ object types, and you have the most complete robot vacuum package money can buy. The self-maintaining dock empties, washes, dries, and refills automatically. It's expensive, but nothing else can match what it does.
