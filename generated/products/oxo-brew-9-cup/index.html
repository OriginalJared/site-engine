Our Review

The OXO Brew 9-Cup brings SCA-certified brewing into an intuitive, beautifully designed package. Its microprocessor-controlled brewing cycle maintains optimal water temperature throughout extraction, and the rainmaker showerhead evenly saturates the coffee bed. A simple one-button interface with an LED brew-time indicator keeps things dead simple. The double-wall thermal carafe pours cleanly and keeps coffee hot. It's the drip machine for design-conscious coffee lovers.
