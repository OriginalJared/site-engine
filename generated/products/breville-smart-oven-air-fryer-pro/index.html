Our Review

The Breville Smart Oven Air Fryer Pro is less an air fryer and more a countertop kitchen replacement. With 13 cooking functions including air fry, slow cook, dehydrate, proof, bake, roast, broil, toast, bagel, pizza, cookies, reheat, and warm, it can handle virtually any cooking task. Element IQ technology directs heat precisely where needed, and the 1 cu ft interior fits a 14-lb turkey. If you want one appliance that does everything, this is the pinnacle.
