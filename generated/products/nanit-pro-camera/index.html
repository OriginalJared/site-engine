Our Review

The Nanit Pro is the Tesla of baby monitors. Its overhead camera provides a bird's-eye view of your baby's crib, while AI-powered sleep tracking analyzes sleep patterns and gives you a nightly Sleep Score. The Breathing Wear system (sold separately) monitors breathing motion through your baby's clothing — no chest clips or sensors touching skin. 1080p HD streaming, background audio, split-screen for two cameras, and real-time alerts make it the most data-rich monitor available.

✅ What We Like

  • Sleep tracking insights are genuinely valuable
  • Breathing Wear is non-contact and non-invasive
  • Overhead view covers the entire crib
  • App is polished and feature-rich
