Our Review

The Hamilton Beach FlexBrew Trio packs surprising versatility into a budget package. It brews K-Cup pods on one side and a full 12-cup carafe on the other — simultaneously if you want. The single-serve side is compatible with K-Cups and ground coffee (with the included single-serve pack), and the carafe side has programmable auto-brew. At under $90, it's the cheapest way to get dual-brew flexibility without sacrificing a full-size carafe.

✅ What We Like

  • Under $90 for dual-brew capability
  • Brews K-Cups AND ground coffee
  • Programmable timer for morning convenience
  • Full 12-cup carafe for families

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Glass carafe — no thermal option
