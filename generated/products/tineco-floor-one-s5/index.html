Our Review

The Tineco Floor One S5 isn't just a vacuum — it's a wet-dry floor washer that vacuums and mops in a single pass. iLoop smart sensors detect dirty spots and automatically boost suction and water flow, while the self-cleaning cycle cleans the brush roller and tubes after each use. It's purpose-built for hard floors (tile, hardwood, laminate) and delivers results that a regular vacuum plus a separate mop can't match.

✅ What We Like

  • Vacuums and mops in one pass — huge time saver
  • iLoop sensor auto-boosts on dirty spots
  • Self-cleaning keeps the machine fresh
  • Excellent on hard floors
