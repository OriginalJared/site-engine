Apple AirPods Max 2

The best headphones for Apple users — USB-C, adaptive audio, and the deepest integration with iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Our Review

The AirPods Max 2 finally address the original's biggest criticism: the Lightning port is gone, replaced by USB-C. Adaptive Audio intelligently blends ANC and transparency based on your environment — walk into a noisy cafe and it adjusts automatically. The H2 chip enables Personalized Spatial Audio with head tracking for immersive movie and music experiences. The anodized aluminum cups and stainless steel frame look and feel premium. At $549, they're expensive, but for users deep in the Apple ecosystem, the seamless switching between iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV is unmatched.
