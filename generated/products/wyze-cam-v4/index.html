Our Review

The Wyze Cam v4 is proof that good security doesn't have to be expensive. At $36, it delivers 2.5K resolution, color night vision with a built-in spotlight, IP65 weather resistance for indoor or outdoor use, and free local storage via microSD card. Motion detection zones are customizable, and two-way audio lets you communicate through the camera. The Wyze app provides live viewing, playback, and smart alerts. No subscription is required for basic features. With 10K+ bought monthly on Amazon, it's the most popular budget security camera for a reason.
