Our Review

The Lectric XP4 750 is the e-bike that changed the market. At under $1,000, it packs a 750W motor, 48V battery with 55+ mile range, fat tires for all-terrain capability, and a folding frame that fits in a car trunk. GearLab rates it their top value pick after lab-testing over 100 e-bikes. The step-through frame accommodates all riders, the pedal assist is smooth with 5 levels, and the throttle provides instant power when needed. It can even carry a passenger on the rear rack. For commuting, camping, or just cruising, nothing else delivers this much bike for this little money.
