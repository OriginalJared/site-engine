Our Review

L-shaped standing desks are rare and usually expensive, which is what makes the FEZIBO 63-inch special. At $350, it gives you a massive 63" x 55" corner workspace with electric height adjustment — something most competitors charge $600+ for. The single motor handles 176 lbs, which is adequate for a spread-out setup across the L-shape. The height range is a bit limited compared to straight desks, but for a corner office where you need every inch of surface area, this is the only real option under $400. FEZIBO includes a monitor stand riser and basic cable tray.
