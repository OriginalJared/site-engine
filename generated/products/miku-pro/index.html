Our Review

The Miku Pro monitors your baby's breathing without any wearable device at all. Its SensorFusion technology uses the camera itself to detect breathing patterns, sleep position, and room conditions — no sock, no clip, no mat under the mattress. 1080p HD video with night vision, real-time breathing visualization in the app, and cry/sound detection provide comprehensive monitoring. If you want breathing data without putting anything on your baby, Miku is the only option.

✅ What We Like

  • 1080p video quality is excellent
  • No ongoing subscription required
