Our Review

The HelloBaby HB6550 proves you don't need to spend $300 for a good baby monitor. For under $70, you get a 5" color display, infrared night vision, two-way audio, temperature monitoring, and up to 4 camera support. The 1000-foot range covers most homes, and 8 lullabies help soothe your baby remotely. It's not smart, it's not fancy, and that's the point — it reliably shows you your baby, and it costs less than dinner for two.
