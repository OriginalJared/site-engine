Our Review

The Acer Aspire Go 15 proves you can get a perfectly usable laptop for under $300. The AMD Ryzen 3 7320U handles web browsing, Office documents, and streaming without issue. The 15.6" Full HD display is sharp for the price, 8GB of RAM keeps multitasking smooth, and the 128GB SSD boots Windows in seconds. It's not glamorous, but for students, basic office work, or a secondary machine, it's unbeatable value.

✅ What We Like

  • Under $300 for a functional laptop
  • Full HD IPS display — no 768p compromise
  • 11-hour battery is strong for the price
  • Lightweight at 3.75 lbs

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 128GB SSD fills up fast
  • Plastic build feels cheap
  • No backlit keyboard
  • Integrated graphics only — no gaming
