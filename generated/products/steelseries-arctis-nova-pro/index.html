Our Review

The Arctis Nova Pro Wireless is the headset gamers and remote workers dream about. Dual wireless lets you connect to your PC/console via low-latency 2.4GHz AND your phone via Bluetooth simultaneously — game audio and phone calls at the same time. The hot-swappable battery system means zero downtime: swap a dead battery for a charged one in seconds. ANC is effective for both gaming immersion and office noise reduction. The retractable ClearCast Gen 2 mic is certified Discord-compatible with AI noise reduction. Premium drivers deliver detailed spatial audio for competitive gaming.
