Our Review

The Sonos Roam 2 is unique: it works as both a Bluetooth portable speaker and a Wi-Fi smart speaker that integrates with your Sonos home system. Take it to the park on Bluetooth, bring it home and it auto-switches to Wi-Fi for multi-room audio. Trueplay tuning adapts sound to your environment, and Alexa/Google Assistant voice control is built in. It's the smartest portable speaker money can buy.
