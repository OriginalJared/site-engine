Our Review

The Q10 S5+ brings Roborock's excellent navigation and build quality down to an accessible price point. LiDAR mapping is fast and accurate, the app is the same polished experience as their $800 models, and the self-emptying dock holds weeks of debris. At 5500Pa suction, it won't match the flagships on raw power, but for daily maintenance of hardwood and low-pile carpet, it's more than enough. The rubber main brush resists hair tangles better than bristle alternatives. If you want the Roborock ecosystem without the premium price, this is your entry point.
