Our Review

The Dell UltraSharp U2723QE sets the standard for professional 4K monitors. Its IPS Black panel delivers a 2000:1 contrast ratio — double typical IPS monitors — for deeper blacks and richer colors. Factory calibrated to Delta E < 2, it's ready for color-critical work out of the box. The built-in USB-C hub with 90W Power Delivery, RJ45 Ethernet, and KVM switch make it a one-cable docking station for your laptop.

✅ What We Like

  • IPS Black panel has noticeably deeper blacks
  • USB-C hub replaces a separate dock
  • Excellent factory calibration
  • Premium build with thin bezels

⚠️ Worth Noting
