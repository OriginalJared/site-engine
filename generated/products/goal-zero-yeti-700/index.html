Our Review

Goal Zero pioneered portable solar power, and the Yeti 700 carries that legacy forward. 697Wh of lithium-ion capacity and 600W output handle camping, overlanding, and emergency use. What sets it apart is build quality — the Yeti feels like a tool, not an appliance. The stackable design lets you daisy-chain multiple units, and the integrated MPPT solar controller maximizes efficiency from Goal Zero's solar panels. It's heavier than competitors (18.3 lbs for its capacity) and lacks LiFePO4, but for users who prioritize durability and the Goal Zero ecosystem, it's the right choice. Outdoor Life's 'Most Reliable' pick.
