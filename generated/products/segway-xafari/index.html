Our Review

The Segway Xafari is what happens when a tech company builds a bicycle. GearLab gave it their highest score ever (93/100). Auto-shifting adjusts gears based on your speed and terrain — no shifter needed. Integrated rear radar warns you of approaching vehicles. The full-color display shows navigation, fitness data, and bike diagnostics. The 1000W mid-drive motor with torque sensor provides the most natural riding experience tested. 75+ mile range handles multi-day touring. At $3,499, it's the most expensive bike on this list, but it's also the most technically advanced e-bike ever produced.
