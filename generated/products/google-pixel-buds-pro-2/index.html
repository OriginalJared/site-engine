Our Review

Google's Pixel Buds Pro 2 are the most comfortable earbuds in this roundup — their small, rounded design with stabilizer fins stays put during workouts without creating ear fatigue during long listening sessions. The headline feature is Gemini AI integration — ask questions, get translations, and control your phone hands-free with natural conversation. ANC is solid (not Bose-level, but effective), and the sound signature is balanced and natural. Google's Find My Device integration means you'll never lose them. At $229, they're for Android users who want the cleanest, most integrated experience.

✅ What We Like

  • Most comfortable earbuds tested — all-day wearable
  • Gemini AI integration is genuinely useful
  • Conversation Detection works flawlessly
  • Excellent Google/Android integration

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • ANC trails Sony and Bose
  • Sound quality is good but not audiophile-grade
  • $229 for mid-tier ANC is steep
  • Limited features on iOS
