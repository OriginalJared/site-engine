Our Review

The ASUS ProArt PA279CRV is built for photographers, video editors, and designers who demand accurate colors. It covers 99% DCI-P3 and 99% Adobe RGB, comes Calman Verified for out-of-box accuracy, and supports hardware calibration via the ProArt Calibration app. USB-C with 96W Power Delivery and a built-in KVM switch add practical productivity features at a price that undercuts Dell and BenQ rivals.

✅ What We Like

  • Outstanding color accuracy at this price
  • 96W USB-C charges even large laptops
  • Hardware calibration maintains accuracy over time
  • Covers both DCI-P3 and Adobe RGB

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 60Hz — not suitable for gaming
  • OSD menu navigation is clunky
  • Slightly less contrast than Dell IPS Black
