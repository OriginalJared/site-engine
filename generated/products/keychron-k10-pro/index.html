The K10 Pro fills a gap: a full-size wireless mechanical keyboard with enthusiast features at a reasonable price. Hot-swappable switches, QMK/VIA firmware, Bluetooth to 3 devices, and a full 104-key layout with numpad — all for $115. The aluminum bezel adds a premium touch to the otherwise plastic case. For anyone who needs a numpad and wants wireless with full programmability, this is the pick.
