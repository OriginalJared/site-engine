Our Review

The K10 Pro fills a gap: a full-size wireless mechanical keyboard with enthusiast features at a reasonable price. Hot-swappable switches, QMK/VIA firmware, Bluetooth to 3 devices, and a full 104-key layout with numpad — all for $115. The aluminum bezel adds a premium touch to the otherwise plastic case. The pre-lubed K Pro switches are smooth, and the OSA-profile PBT keycaps have a satisfying sculpted feel. Battery life reaches 200+ hours with backlight off. For anyone who needs a numpad and wants wireless with full programmability, this is the pick.
