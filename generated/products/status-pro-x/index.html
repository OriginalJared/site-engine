Status Pro X Wireless Earbuds

Forbes' #1 pick — the best balance of sound, ANC, and battery life at a mid-range price.

★ 4.6 / 5 $130
Check Price on Amazon Compare All Wireless Earbuds

Our Review

The Status Pro X came out of nowhere to claim Forbes' top pick for 2026. The 12mm drivers deliver remarkably detailed sound with a balanced signature that works across all genres. ANC is effective without the pressure feeling some competitors create. The standout is battery life: 10 hours per charge with ANC on, and 40 hours total with the case. Physical buttons (not touch controls) mean no accidental taps, and the IP55 rating handles workouts and rain. At $130, they undercut the big three (Sony, Bose, Apple) by $120+ while delivering 90% of the experience.
