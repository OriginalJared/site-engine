Our Review

The Ninja DualBrew Pro does it all: K-Cup pods, ground coffee, and specialty concentrate for lattes and cappuccinos. It brews a single cup or a full 12-cup carafe, with separate brew baskets for pods and grounds. The Fold-Away frother creates creamy milk foam for specialty drinks, and the adjustable brew strength (Classic, Rich, Over Ice, Specialty) means every cup is customized to your taste. It's the Swiss Army knife of coffee makers.

✅ What We Like

  • Brews both K-Cups and ground coffee
  • Built-in frother for specialty drinks
  • 12-cup thermal carafe keeps coffee hot
  • 4 brew styles cover every preference

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Large footprint on the counter
  • K-Cup brew is average — not as good as ground
  • Frother can be finicky with plant milks
  • Lots of parts to clean
