Our Review

Most mechanical keyboards scream 'gamer,' but the Razer Pro Type Ultra is built for the office. The silent Yellow mechanical switches have the tactile feel of mechanical keys without the clicky noise that annoys coworkers. The clean white-and-silver design blends into any professional workspace. Bluetooth connects to up to 4 devices simultaneously, and the USB dongle provides a low-latency connection for your primary machine. The ergonomic wrist rest is included, and the white backlight is subtle and functional. Razer Synapse software offers full macro programming. PCMag's top pick for office mechanical keyboards.
