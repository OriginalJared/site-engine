Our Review

The TP-Link Deco XE75 brings Wi-Fi 6E to the masses at a price that doesn't sting. The 3-pack blankets up to 7,200 sq ft with tri-band coverage, using the dedicated 6GHz band as a wireless backhaul to keep your 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands free for devices. AI-Driven Mesh intelligently routes traffic, and the Deco app makes setup a breeze. With 200+ device support and built-in HomeShield security, it's the complete package.

✅ What We Like

  • Wi-Fi 6E at a very competitive price
  • Dedicated 6GHz backhaul keeps other bands fast
  • Covers large homes with no dead zones
  • Deco app setup takes under 10 minutes

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 6GHz range is shorter than 5GHz
  • No USB port for NAS sharing
  • HomeShield premium features require subscription
