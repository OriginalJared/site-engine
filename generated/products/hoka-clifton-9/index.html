Our Review

The HOKA Clifton 9 is the shoe that made max cushioning mainstream. Despite its thick, pillowy midsole, it weighs just 8.9 oz — lighter than many traditional trainers. The compression-molded EVA foam is soft without being unstable, and HOKA's signature meta-rocker geometry creates a rolling sensation that propels you forward. The breathable engineered mesh upper and early-stage Meta-Rocker make every run feel easier than it should.

✅ What We Like

  • Max cushion at under 9 oz is remarkable
  • Meta-rocker makes runs feel effortless
  • 5mm drop encourages natural gait
  • Soft enough for recovery, light enough for tempo

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Outsole durability is average
  • Not ideal for technical trails
  • Cushioning compresses over time
  • Narrow fit for wide feet

Key Features
