Our Review

The Aventon Level 3 is GearLab's top-rated everyday e-bike, and riding it you understand why. Unlike cheaper hub-motor bikes, the Level 3 uses a torque sensor that responds to how hard you pedal — it feels natural, like the wind is always at your back. Integrated front and rear lights, fenders, and a rear rack make it commute-ready out of the box. The 750W motor handles hills effortlessly, and the 60+ mile range covers a full week of commuting on one charge. The step-through frame, hydraulic disc brakes, and Shimano 8-speed drivetrain make this feel like a premium bicycle that happens to be electric.
