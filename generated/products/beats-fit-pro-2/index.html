Our Review

Beats Fit Pro 2 solve the biggest problem with workout earbuds: they stay put. The flexible wingtip design locks into your ear's concha ridge, and they won't budge during running, jumping, or weight training. Powered by Apple's H2 chip, they get ANC, transparency mode, and spatial audio on both iOS and Android (most Apple chip earbuds limit Android features). Sound is punchy and bass-forward — perfect for workout playlists. 6 hours of battery with ANC on, and a 5-minute quick charge gives an hour of playback. If fitness is your primary use case, these are the ones.
