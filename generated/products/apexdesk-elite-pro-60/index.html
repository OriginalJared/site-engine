Our Review

Finding a 60-inch standing desk under $500 with dual motors is tough, but the ApexDesk Elite Pro pulls it off. The frame is solid and handles 235 lbs, and the desktop gives you plenty of room for a multi-monitor layout. The height range starts at 30 inches (higher than some competitors), which could be a drawback for shorter users, but the 49-inch max is standard. ApexDesk includes a 5-year warranty and a straightforward assembly process. If you want a big desk on a moderate budget, this is the value pick.

✅ What We Like

  • Exceptional value — 60" dual-motor desk under $500
  • Spacious desktop for multi-monitor setups
  • Solid build quality for the price
  • Straightforward assembly

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 30" min height is too high for shorter users
