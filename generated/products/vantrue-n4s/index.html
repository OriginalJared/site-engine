Our Review

The Vantrue N4S covers every angle with three cameras: front (2.7K), cabin-facing (1440p with IR night vision), and rear (1440p). This makes it ideal for Uber/Lyft drivers, taxi operators, and fleet managers who need interior footage. The infrared cabin camera captures clear footage even in complete darkness — passengers are clearly visible. Sony STARVIS 2 sensors in all three cameras ensure quality across the board. 24-hour parking mode with buffered recording catches vandals and hit-and-runs. The 2-inch display is compact, and the app handles multi-camera footage management.
