Our Review

The ROG Zephyrus G14 defies the gaming laptop stereotype. At just 3.3 lbs and 0.63" thin, it looks like an ultrabook but packs an AMD Ryzen 9 8945HS and NVIDIA RTX 4060 for serious gaming performance. The 14" 3K OLED display with 120Hz refresh rate delivers stunning visuals for both gaming and creative work. 10+ hours of battery life for non-gaming tasks means it doubles as a daily driver. It's the laptop that proves you don't need a tank to game.

✅ What We Like

  • RTX 4060 in a 3.3 lb body is remarkable
  • OLED display is best-in-class for a gaming laptop
  • Doubles as a premium ultrabook for daily use
  • Excellent keyboard and trackpad

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Webcam is only 1080p with no IR
  • Fan noise is audible during gaming
  • RTX 4060 can't max out all AAA games at 3K
  • Soldered RAM — not upgradeable

Key Features

  • AMD Ryzen 9 8945HS
  • NVIDIA RTX 4060 Laptop GPU
  • 14" 3K 120Hz OLED display
  • 16GB DDR5 RAM
  • 1TB PCIe 4.0 SSD
  • 10+ hour battery (non-gaming)
