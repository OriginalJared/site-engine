Our Review

If sound quality is your top priority, the Sennheiser Momentum 4 wins. Sennheiser has been making audiophile equipment for 80 years, and that expertise shows. The 42mm drivers deliver the most natural, detailed sound of any wireless headphone — instruments have texture, vocals have presence, and the soundstage is wide and immersive. ANC is good (not Bose-level, but effective), and 60 hours of battery life is absurd — charge once every two weeks. The foldable design and premium materials make them travel-ready. At $300 (down from $350 launch), they're the value pick for audio enthusiasts.
