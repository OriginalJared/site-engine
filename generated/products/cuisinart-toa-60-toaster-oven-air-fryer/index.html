Our Review

The Cuisinart TOA-60 combines a full-size toaster oven with a powerful air fryer in one stainless steel package. It fits 6 slices of bread or a 12-inch pizza, and the air fry function delivers crispy results with the same convection heating. Seven cooking functions (air fry, convection bake, convection broil, bake, broil, warm, toast) make it the ultimate countertop multitasker. If you want one appliance to rule them all, this is it.
