Our Review

The BenQ HT2060 proves you don't need 4K for a great home theater experience. Its 1080p DLP engine with HDR10 and HLG support produces a sharp, color-accurate image that fills a 120" screen beautifully. BenQ's CinematicColor technology covers 96% of Rec.709 for film-accurate colors, and 2,300 lumens handles moderately lit rooms. Lens shift and 1.3x zoom provide flexible placement. At under $700, it's the best entry into dedicated home theater projection.

✅ What We Like

  • Outstanding image quality for 1080p
  • CinematicColor is film-accurate
  • Lens shift is rare at this price
  • Great starting point for home theater

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Only 1080p — no 4K
  • DLP rainbow effect possible
  • No smart TV built-in
  • Fan noise in full brightness mode
