Our Review

The Anker SOLIX C1000 Gen 2 delivers flagship specs at a mid-range price. 1056Wh of LiFePO4 capacity, 2000W continuous output (2400W surge), and a 58-minute wall charge time make it competitive with stations costing $200 more. GearLab rated it 'Best for Most People' thanks to its balance of price, power, and portability. The Anker app provides remote monitoring and firmware updates. At frequently-on-sale prices under $400, it's the best value in the 1000Wh class. 5-year warranty from one of the most trusted names in charging.
