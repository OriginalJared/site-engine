Our Review

The Razer Viper V3 Pro pushes the boundaries of competitive gaming mice with a stunning 54g weight and the Focus Pro 36K sensor. Its symmetrical ambidextrous shape works for claw and fingertip grips, while the new Gen-3 optical switches deliver zero-bounce actuation. With 95 hours of wireless battery life and 8000Hz HyperPolling support, this is Razer's flagship without compromise.

✅ What We Like

  • 54g is absurdly light for a full-featured mouse
  • 8000Hz polling for the smoothest tracking possible
  • Excellent build quality despite low weight
  • Works for both claw and fingertip grips

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Very expensive at $159
  • Ambidextrous shape may not suit palm grip
  • Side buttons only on left side
