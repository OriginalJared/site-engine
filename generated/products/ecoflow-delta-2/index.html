Our Review

The EcoFlow DELTA 2 is the power station reviewers keep recommending. Its X-Stream technology charges from 0 to 80% in just 50 minutes via wall outlet — no other station in this class comes close. X-Boost technology lets it power appliances drawing up to 2200W despite a rated 1800W output, meaning it handles everything from full-size fridges to power tools. The LiFePO4 battery is rated for 3,000+ cycles, and the EcoFlow app provides real-time monitoring and control. At 27 lbs, it's portable enough for car camping while powerful enough for home backup. Outdoor Life and GearLab's top pick.
