Our Review

The Corsair M75 Air Wireless enters the ultralight arena with a 60g ergonomic body, the Marksman 26K sensor, and Corsair's SLIPSTREAM wireless technology. Its comfortable right-hand shape sets it apart from the ambidextrous ultralights, making it ideal for palm and relaxed claw grip users who want minimal weight without sacrificing comfort. Up to 100 hours of battery life seals the deal.

✅ What We Like

  • 60g in a comfortable ergonomic shape is rare
  • SLIPSTREAM wireless is low-latency
  • Great for palm grip users wanting ultralight
  • Solid 100-hour battery

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • No USB-C dongle storage in mouse
  • Side grips could be grippier
  • iCUE software can be bloated
