Our Review

The Ring Indoor Cam Plus is the simplest way to monitor inside your home. At $50, it delivers 2K video, a physical privacy shutter (flip it closed when you're home), two-way audio, and motion detection. The compact size fits on any shelf or mounts on a wall. Ring's app provides live viewing, event history, and the ability to share clips. Pre-roll video capture shows what happened before motion triggered — crucial for understanding events. It's wired (USB-C), so no battery concerns. The most popular indoor camera on Amazon with 40,000+ reviews.
