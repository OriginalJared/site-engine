Our Review

Samsung's Freestyle 2nd Gen is a projector, smart speaker, and ambient light all in one ultra-portable package. It projects up to 100" in Full HD from nearly any angle, auto-levels and auto-focuses instantly, and runs Samsung's Tizen smart TV OS with every major streaming app built in. The 360° speaker sounds surprisingly good, and the included battery base lets you use it completely wire-free outdoors. It's not a home theater replacement — it's the ultimate portable entertainment device.

✅ What We Like

  • Ultra-portable — take it anywhere
  • Tizen OS has every streaming app
  • 360° speaker doubles as a Bluetooth speaker
  • Auto setup is instant

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Only 1080p — no 4K
  • 550 lumens is dim — needs a dark room
  • Battery base sold separately
  • Not bright enough for daytime viewing
