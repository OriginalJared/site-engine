Our Review

The Bose SoundLink Max is Bose's flagship portable speaker, delivering the brand's signature clear, detailed sound at serious volume levels. Dual custom transducers and dual passive radiators produce deep, articulate bass that stays clean even at max volume. The 20-hour battery, IP67 rating, Bose SimpleSync, and Bluetooth 5.3 with Auracast make it the premium choice for discerning listeners who want the best sound quality in a portable package.

✅ What We Like

  • 20-hour battery in a big speaker is impressive
  • Aux input for wired connections
