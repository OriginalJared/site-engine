Our Review

The Weber Spirit II E-310 is the gas grill that grilling experts recommend more than any other. Its GS4 grilling system — Infinity Ignition, burners, Flavorizer bars, and grease management — is engineered for consistent, flavorful results every time. Three burners provide 529 sq in of cooking space (enough for 15 burgers), and the open-cart design with side tables keeps tools within reach. Weber's 10-year warranty and massive accessory ecosystem seal the deal.
