Our Review

When you need to clean a massive space — open-concept living rooms, lofts, basements — the Blue Pure 211i Max delivers. It processes 3,048 sq ft of air per hour using Blueair's proprietary HEPASilent technology, which combines mechanical and electrostatic filtration to capture 99.97% of particles while maintaining lower noise and energy use than pure HEPA systems. The app provides real-time AQI monitoring, auto mode, and filter life tracking. The washable pre-filter comes in multiple colors so you can match your decor. At $300, it's the large-room king.

✅ What We Like

  • Massive coverage area — best for large/open spaces
  • HEPASilent runs quieter than pure HEPA at same output
  • App and auto mode work reliably
  • Washable pre-filter reduces ongoing costs

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Large unit — takes significant floor space
  • Replacement filters are expensive (~$70)
  • HEPASilent ionizer component may concern some users
  • Overkill for bedrooms or small rooms
