Our Review

The LG 27GP950-B is designed for gamers who want the best HDR experience at 4K. Its Nano IPS panel delivers 98% DCI-P3 coverage with DisplayHDR 600 certification for genuine HDR impact. HDMI 2.1 with 4K@120Hz support makes it ideal for PS5 and Xbox Series X, while 160Hz over DisplayPort handles PC gaming. NVIDIA G-Sync Compatible and AMD FreeSync Premium Pro keep things tear-free.
