Our Review

The Oral-B Pro 1000 is the electric toothbrush that dentists and review sites recommend more than any other. It uses oscillating-rotating-pulsating action to remove 300% more plaque along the gumline than a manual brush, and it comes with a pressure sensor, 2-minute timer with 30-second quadrant alerts, and 3 cleaning modes. There are no apps, no Bluetooth, no gimmicks — just a reliable, proven brush at a price that's hard to argue with. If you're switching from manual to electric for the first time, start here.
