Our Review

The VANKYO Leisure 470 Pro delivers a surprisingly watchable picture for under $160. Native 1080p resolution is sharp enough for a 120" screen, and 250 ANSI lumens works well in a dark room. Built-in speakers, HDMI/USB inputs, and a 5W LED lamp that lasts 60,000 hours make it a simple, affordable choice for casual movie nights, kids' rooms, or outdoor screenings. It's not competing with Epson or BenQ — it's competing with your TV, and at this price, it wins on screen size.

✅ What We Like

  • Under $160 for a 1080p projector
  • 60,000-hour lamp never needs replacement
  • Simple setup with multiple inputs
  • Great for casual use and outdoor movies

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 250 lumens requires a dark room
  • No smart TV — needs external streaming device
  • Color accuracy is basic
  • Fan noise is noticeable
