Our Review

The HP Z27k G3 is built for corporate environments and power users who need a reliable 4K display with enterprise connectivity. Its USB-C hub with 100W Power Delivery, RJ45 Ethernet, and four USB-A ports replaces a docking station entirely. Factory calibrated for sRGB accuracy, VESA-mountable, and backed by HP's business warranty, it's the monitor IT departments love to deploy.
