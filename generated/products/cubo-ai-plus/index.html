Our Review

The Cubo Ai Plus uses computer vision AI to detect potentially dangerous situations that other monitors miss. It alerts you when your baby's face is covered by a blanket, when they roll over to a face-down position, or when they enter a designated danger zone in the crib. 1080p HD video with 18-hour playback, temperature/humidity sensors, and cry detection add standard smart features on top of the AI safety layer. It's the monitor for safety-conscious parents.
