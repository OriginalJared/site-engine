Our Review

The Xiaomi Electric Scooter 4 Pro delivers premium performance at a mid-range price. Its 700W peak motor pushes to 20 mph, and the 446Wh battery provides up to 30 miles of range. The 10" tubeless pneumatic tires are puncture-resistant, and dual braking (regenerative + disc) stops you quickly and safely. The minimalist design with an LED dashboard, app connectivity, and cruise control makes it the most refined scooter in its price range.

✅ What We Like

  • 30-mile range at $799 is excellent value
  • Tubeless tires resist punctures
  • Dual braking provides confident stopping
  • Sleek, minimalist design

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 20 mph top speed is moderate
  • Heavy at 37 lbs
  • No suspension — relies on tire absorption
  • Charging takes 9 hours
