Our Review

The Bonavita Connoisseur delivers SCA-certified brewing at nearly half the price of a Moccamaster. Its 1500W heater reaches the optimal 195-205°F range quickly, and a wide showerhead distributes water evenly over the coffee bed for balanced extraction. The pre-infusion mode blooms grounds before full brewing — a technique borrowed from manual pour-over. The thermal carafe keeps coffee hot for hours without a hot plate burning it. Simple, effective, and fairly priced.
