Our Review

The Logitech G305 proves you don't need to spend $150 for wireless gaming. Powered by the HERO sensor and Logitech's LIGHTSPEED wireless technology, it delivers lag-free performance indistinguishable from wired mice. A single AA battery lasts up to 250 hours, and the compact shape suits claw and fingertip grips perfectly. It's the best value in wireless gaming mice, period.
