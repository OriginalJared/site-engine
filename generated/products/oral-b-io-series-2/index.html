Our Review

The iO Series 2 brings Oral-B's premium iO technology down to an entry-level price. It combines powerful micro-vibrations with the signature round brush head to deliver 100% more plaque removal versus a manual toothbrush. The automatic pressure sensor slows the brush and signals red if you push too hard, protecting your gums from day one. You get 3 cleaning modes, a 2-minute timer, and 2 brush heads in the box. It's the smart on-ramp to the iO ecosystem without the $200+ price tag of the higher-end models.
