Our Review

The WH-CH720N brings Sony's noise cancellation technology down to an accessible price. The same Integrated Processor V1 from previous flagship models handles ANC duties, and the sound quality carries Sony's signature warm, detailed tuning. At just 192g, they're the lightest headphones on this list — you barely feel them during long sessions. 35 hours of battery with ANC, multipoint Bluetooth for two devices, and Alexa/Google Assistant support round out a compelling mid-range package. At under $100 on sale, they're the sweet spot between budget and premium.

✅ What We Like

  • Sony ANC quality at under $100
  • Lightest headphones — barely noticeable
  • 35-hour battery is excellent
  • Multipoint Bluetooth is convenient

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • No LDAC support (AAC only)
  • Plastic build feels budget
  • No foldable design
