Our Review

The ASUS ZenWiFi AX XT8 has been the go-to reliable mesh system since its launch and continues to deliver outstanding performance. Tri-band AX6600 with a dedicated 5GHz backhaul ensures satellites maintain full speed, and AiProtection Pro provides free lifetime network security without any subscription. With coverage up to 5,500 sq ft and a 2.5G WAN port, it remains one of the best values in tri-band mesh networking.
