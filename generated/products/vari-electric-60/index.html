Our Review

Vari is the brand you see in corporate offices across the country, and the Electric 60x30 is their flagship home model. The 10-year warranty is the longest in this roundup, signaling serious confidence in their build quality. The desk spans 25 to 50.5 inches in height and features a clean, tool-free T-leg design that simplifies assembly. The 200 lb weight capacity is lower than some competitors, but it's more than enough for a standard dual-monitor setup. If long-term reliability and brand trust matter most to you, Vari is a safe bet.
