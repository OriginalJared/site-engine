Our Review

The HP Spectre x360 14 is the most elegant 2-in-1 on the market. Its 360-degree hinge transforms it from a traditional laptop to a tablet or tent-mode display, and the 14" 2.8K OLED touchscreen with pen support makes it a creative powerhouse. Intel Core Ultra processors, 16GB RAM, and a gem-cut design with diamond-cut edges make it feel genuinely premium. If you want a laptop that also works as a drawing tablet, this is the one.

✅ What We Like

  • Stunning gem-cut design is head-turning
  • OLED touchscreen with pen support
  • Versatile 2-in-1 form factor
  • Includes rechargeable stylus in the box
