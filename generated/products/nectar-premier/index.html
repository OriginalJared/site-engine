Our Review

The Nectar Premier is for memory foam lovers who don't want to sleep hot. Its gel-infused memory foam layer contours to your body for excellent pressure relief, while the phase-change cooling cover actively regulates surface temperature. The ActiveComfort transition layer adds responsiveness so you don't feel trapped, and the 12" profile provides substantial support. A 365-night trial and forever warranty make it a risk-free purchase.
