Our Review

The DEEBOT T30S Omni hits the sweet spot between premium features and reasonable pricing. Its TruEdge adaptive mopping system extends the mop pad to reach right up to baseboards, and 11,000Pa suction handles everything from hardwood dust to embedded carpet dirt. The OMNI station empties the dustbin, washes and dries the mop pads with hot water and hot air, and refills the clean water tank. ECOVACS' YIKO voice assistant lets you give natural language commands without the app. At around $600, it delivers 90% of what $1000+ robots offer.

✅ What We Like

  • Excellent value — premium features under $700
  • TruEdge mopping is genuinely effective
  • Quiet operation for a high-suction robot
  • Voice control without the app is convenient

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Mop pad lifting is limited on thick carpets
  • Dock is still quite large
  • App can be buggy after firmware updates
  • Mapping can struggle in very dark rooms
