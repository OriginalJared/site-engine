Our Review

The Aventon Aventure 3 is built for riders who refuse to stick to pavement. 4-inch fat tires roll over sand, snow, gravel, and mud with confidence, while the torque sensor provides natural pedal assist that adapts to terrain. The 750W motor with 85Nm of torque powers through steep hills, and the 60+ mile range handles full-day adventures. Hydraulic disc brakes provide reliable stopping power even in wet conditions. The integrated display, lights, and fenders make it practical for daily use too. GearLab scored it 87/100 — their highest-rated fat tire e-bike.
