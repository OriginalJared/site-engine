Our Review

eufy has built its brand on one promise: no subscriptions, ever. The SoloCam S350 delivers 4K resolution from dual lenses — a wide-angle lens for monitoring and a telephoto lens with 8x hybrid zoom for detail. The built-in solar panel keeps the battery topped up indefinitely in most climates. All footage stores locally on the 8GB built-in eMMC storage. AI person detection, auto-tracking, and two-way audio work without any monthly fee. For users who refuse to pay subscriptions, eufy is the only premium option.
