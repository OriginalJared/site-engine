Our Review

The Casper Original is the mattress that launched the bed-in-a-box revolution, and it's been refined into a well-rounded choice for almost any sleeper. Three layers of foam — AirScape perforated cooling foam, zoned support foam, and a durable base — create a balanced medium feel that's soft enough for side sleepers yet supportive enough for back sleepers. It's the safe choice: unlikely to blow your mind, but very unlikely to disappoint.

✅ What We Like

  • Balanced feel works for most sleep positions
  • AirScape foam sleeps cooler than solid foam
  • Zoned support helps spinal alignment
  • Trusted brand with easy returns

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Not exceptional in any single category
  • All-foam construction may sleep warm for hot sleepers
  • Edge support is average
  • 100-night trial is shorter than some competitors
