Our Review

The AirPods Pro 3 aren't just earbuds — they're health devices. Built-in heart rate sensors track your pulse during workouts, and the FDA-cleared Hearing Aid mode can amplify ambient sound for users with mild hearing loss. Noise cancellation has been significantly improved over the Pro 2, and Adaptive Audio automatically blends ANC and transparency based on your environment. Five ear tip sizes ensure a good fit, and spatial audio with head tracking creates an immersive listening experience. If you own an iPhone, these are the earbuds to get — nothing else integrates as deeply with iOS.

✅ What We Like

  • Deepest iPhone/Apple ecosystem integration
  • Heart rate + hearing aid features are unique
  • 5 ear tip sizes fit more ears than competitors
  • Adaptive Audio is genuinely useful

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Full features require iPhone — limited on Android
  • No LDAC or high-res codec support
  • $249 is steep for non-audiophile sound
  • Case scratches easily
