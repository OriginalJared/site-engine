Our Review

Narwal made its name with mopping, and the Freo X Ultra shows why. Its unique triangular mop design reaches into corners and along edges where round mop pads leave dirty strips. The DirtSense system automatically detects dirty areas and re-mops them until the sensor reads clean. With 12,000Pa suction, it's no slouch as a vacuum either. The all-in-one dock handles emptying, mop washing with 65°C water, and hot air drying. The TwinAI system uses both a camera and LiDAR for the most reliable obstacle avoidance in our testing. Premium price, but if hard floor mopping is your priority, nothing else comes close.
