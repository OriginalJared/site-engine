The Tapo RV30 Max Plus is a budget miracle. For around $230, you get LiDAR navigation (not camera-based or random-bounce), a self-emptying dock, 5300Pa suction, and a basic mopping system. Two years ago, this feature set cost $500+. The app supports multi-floor mapping, no-go zones, and scheduling. Suction won't match the $800+ robots, but for daily maintenance cleaning of hardwood and low-pile carpet, it's more than adequate. TP-Link's smart home ecosystem integration is a bonus if you already use their products.
