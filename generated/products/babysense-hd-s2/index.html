Our Review

The Babysense HD S2 is ideal for families with two kids in separate rooms. It supports up to 4 cameras with a split-screen view on the dedicated 5" display, so you can monitor multiple rooms at once. Each camera offers remote pan, tilt, and zoom with 1080p resolution and excellent night vision. The long 960-foot range handles large homes, and the encrypted signal means no Wi-Fi and no hacking risk. It's the multi-room monitoring champion.
