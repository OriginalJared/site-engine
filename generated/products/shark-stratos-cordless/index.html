Our Review

The Shark Stratos Cordless introduces odor-neutralizing technology that other vacuums ignore. Its anti-odor cartridge actively neutralizes odors trapped in the vacuum, while Clean Sense IQ automatically detects hidden debris and boosts suction. DuoClean PowerFins combines a soft roller and bristle brush for both hard floors and carpet, and up to 60 minutes of runtime handles large homes. It's the vacuum for pet owners and anyone tired of smelly dustbins.
