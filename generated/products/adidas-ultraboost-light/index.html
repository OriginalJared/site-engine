Our Review

The Adidas Ultraboost Light is the lightest Ultraboost ever, using 30% less material in the midsole without sacrificing the signature Boost energy return. The result is a shoe that's genuinely good for running — responsive, cushioned, and smooth — while also being one of the most stylish sneakers you can wear with jeans. The Primeknit+ upper is soft and seamless, and Continental rubber on the outsole grips in any weather. It's the rare shoe that excels on the run and on the street.

✅ What We Like

  • Looks incredible as casual wear
  • Boost energy return is legendary
  • Continental outsole grips in wet conditions
  • Lightest Ultraboost ever made

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $190 is expensive
  • Not as performance-focused as pure running shoes
  • Primeknit upper stretches over time
  • Boost foam compresses with heavy use
