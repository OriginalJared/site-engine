Our Review

The On Cloudmonster 2 lives up to its name — it's On's most cushioned shoe with a ride that feels like bouncing on clouds. The CloudTec Phase pods compress sequentially from heel to toe, creating a rolling, springy sensation unlike any other shoe. Helion HF foam is soft yet durable, and the oversized design looks as bold as it runs. For runners who want maximum cushion with a playful, bouncy character, the Cloudmonster 2 delivers a one-of-a-kind experience.
