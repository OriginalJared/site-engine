Our Review

The GOTRAX XR Ultra is the cheapest electric scooter worth buying for daily use. Its 300W motor reaches 15.5 mph, and the 187Wh battery provides up to 17 miles of range — enough for short commutes and errands. The 8.5" pneumatic tires provide a smoother ride than solid alternatives, and the foldable design makes it easy to store under a desk or carry onto a bus. At $349, it's the gateway into electric scooter commuting.

✅ What We Like

  • $349 — cheapest usable commuter scooter
  • 26 lbs is easy to carry
  • Pneumatic tires ride smoother than solids
  • Foldable for easy storage

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 15.5 mph is slow for road use
  • 17-mile range limits longer commutes
  • No suspension at all
  • 8.5" tires feel every bump
