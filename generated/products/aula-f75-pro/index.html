Our Review

The AULA F75 Pro has been Amazon's #1 selling mechanical keyboard, and the value proposition is absurd. For $66 you get a gasket-mounted 75% layout with a volume knob, hot-swappable switches, pre-lubed Reaper linear switches, RGB backlighting, triple connectivity (BT/2.4GHz/USB-C), and PBT keycaps. The sound profile out of the box is surprisingly good — creamy and thocky without any modifications. The build is plastic, but the typing experience rivals keyboards twice its price. 3,000+ units sold per month on Amazon. If you want a great wireless mechanical keyboard without spending $200, this is it.
