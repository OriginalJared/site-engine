Our Review

The Nike Pegasus 41 is the most trusted daily running shoe in the world, and for good reason. The ReactX foam midsole delivers 13% more energy return than React foam while being softer and more responsive underfoot. A redesigned Air Zoom unit in the forefoot adds a snappy toe-off, and the engineered mesh upper breathes while locking your foot in place. Whether you're running 3 miles or 13, the Pegasus handles it all.
