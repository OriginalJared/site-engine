Our Review

The LG 27UL850-W offers a balanced mix of features for users who want 4K quality without overspending. Its IPS panel covers 99% sRGB with HDR10 and VESA DisplayHDR 400 support for improved contrast in HDR content. USB-C with 60W Power Delivery handles laptop charging, while the ergonomic stand provides full adjustability. It's the reliable mid-range choice for mixed use.

✅ What We Like

  • USB-C charging is convenient
