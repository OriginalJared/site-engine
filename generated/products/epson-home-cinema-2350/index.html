Our Review

The Epson Home Cinema 2350 delivers a true cinematic experience at home. Its 3LCD technology produces 2,800 lumens of equal color and white brightness, meaning vibrant images even with some ambient light. 4K PRO-UHD resolution with pixel-shifting creates a sharp, detailed picture, and built-in Android TV with Google Assistant means streaming apps are ready out of the box — no external device needed. Lens shift and keystone correction make setup flexible.

✅ What We Like

  • Bright enough for rooms with some light
  • 3LCD eliminates DLP rainbow effect
  • Android TV built-in is genuinely convenient
  • Flexible placement with lens shift

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Not true native 4K — pixel-shifting
  • Fan noise is audible in quiet scenes
  • No HDR tone mapping
  • Lamp replacement costs ~$200

Key Features

  • 4K PRO-UHD resolution (pixel-shifting)
  • 2,800 lumens color/white brightness
  • 3LCD technology — no rainbow effect
  • Built-in Android TV + Google Assistant
  • Lens shift (vertical + horizontal)
  • Bluetooth audio output
