Our Review

The Sony WF-1000XM6 are the earbuds audiophiles recommend. The new Dynamic Driver X delivers deep, textured bass alongside crystal-clear mids and highs. Noise cancellation rivals over-ear headphones thanks to the V2 processor, and the memory foam tips create a seal that blocks ambient noise before ANC even kicks in. Battery life hits 8 hours with ANC on (24 total with case). The companion app offers a graphic EQ, LDAC high-res streaming, and a 'Background Music' ambiance feature. At $298, they're premium-priced but deliver a listening experience nothing else in this form factor can match.
