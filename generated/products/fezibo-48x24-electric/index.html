Our Review

At $200, the FEZIBO 48x24 is the entry point to electric standing desks. It won't win any awards for capacity (176 lbs) or height range, but it does the core job well: it goes up, it goes down, and it's stable enough for a single monitor and laptop. The single motor is slower and louder than dual-motor models, but for a home office or study where you just want the option to stand, it's a steal. FEZIBO includes a splice board for simple cable management and a 2-year warranty.
