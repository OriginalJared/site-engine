Our Review

The Breville Oracle Jet is the most advanced home espresso machine you can buy. It automates every step — grinding, dosing, tamping, extraction, and milk texturing — while still allowing manual override for each parameter. The dual boiler system means you can extract and steam simultaneously (no waiting). The ThermoJet heating system reaches temperature in 3 seconds. The 6-inch touchscreen displays extraction pressure in real-time. At $2,500, it's a serious investment, but it produces shots that rival commercial cafe machines. For the home barista who wants the absolute best, this is the endgame.
