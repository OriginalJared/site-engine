Our Review

The Optoma UHD38x delivers flagship specs at a mid-range price. True 4K UHD resolution with 4,000 lumens means a bright, sharp image even in well-lit rooms — the brightest in this roundup. 240Hz refresh rate support with 4.2ms input lag at 1080p makes it genuinely competitive for gaming. The DLP chip with 8.3 million on-screen pixels produces razor-sharp detail, and the 1.1–1.3x zoom provides placement flexibility. It's the best spec-for-dollar 4K projector available.

✅ What We Like

  • 4,000 lumens is exceptionally bright
  • 4.2ms input lag is excellent for gaming
  • True 4K at this price is outstanding value
  • 15,000-hour lamp life reduces replacement cost

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • DLP rainbow effect may bother sensitive viewers
  • No smart TV built-in
  • No lens shift — keystone only
  • Fan is audible in full brightness mode
