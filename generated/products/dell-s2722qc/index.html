Our Review

The Dell S2722QC proves 4K doesn't have to be expensive. For just $309, you get a crisp 27" 4K IPS panel with USB-C connectivity and 65W Power Delivery — enough to charge most ultrabooks. AMD FreeSync support handles light gaming, and the fully adjustable stand tilts, swivels, pivots, and adjusts height. It's the best entry point into 4K for office workers and students.

✅ What We Like

  • Incredible value at $309 for 4K USB-C
  • 65W USB-C charges most laptops
  • Fully adjustable stand included
  • Sharp, clear 4K image quality

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • 60Hz only — not for serious gaming
  • Colors are decent but not pro-grade
  • 65W may not charge larger laptops
