Our Review

The Helix Midnight Luxe is engineered specifically for side sleepers. Its zoned lumbar support system provides firmer support under your hips and softer cushioning under your shoulders — exactly where side sleepers need it. The Tencel pillow top adds a luxury surface feel, and over 1,000 individually wrapped coils minimize motion transfer so your partner's tossing doesn't wake you. GlacioTex cooling cover keeps the surface cool all night.
