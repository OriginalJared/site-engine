Our Review

The TP-Link Deco BE65 is one of the first Wi-Fi 7 mesh systems at a price that isn't stratospheric. BE11000 tri-band speeds, Multi-Link Operation (MLO) for simultaneous multi-band connections, and 320 MHz channel support deliver genuinely next-gen performance. The 3-pack covers up to 6,500 sq ft, and a 2.5G Ethernet port on each node handles faster internet plans. If you're future-proofing your home network, this is the entry point.
