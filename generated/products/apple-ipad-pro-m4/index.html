Our Review

The iPad Pro M4 is the most powerful tablet ever created — and it's not close. The M4 chip benchmarks faster than many MacBook Pros, the Ultra Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion 120Hz is the best screen on any mobile device, and at just 5.1mm thin, it's thinner than an iPod nano. Professional video editors, 3D artists, and music producers use this as their primary creation device. Thunderbolt connectivity, Face ID, and up to 2TB storage complete the picture. At $1,299, it's expensive, but it's also the only tablet that can truly replace a laptop for professional creative work.
