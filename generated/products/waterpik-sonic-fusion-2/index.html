Our Review

The Waterpik Sonic-Fusion 2.0 solves the biggest problem in oral care: nobody flosses enough. It combines a sonic electric toothbrush with Waterpik's proven water flosser technology in one device. Three modes — Brush, Floss, or Brush+Floss — let you customize your routine with one hand. It's clinically proven to be 2x as effective as traditional brushing and flossing combined for reducing plaque and improving gum health. The 10 pressure settings, 60-second floss reservoir, and ADA Seal of Acceptance make this the ultimate all-in-one for people who want thorough oral care without juggling multiple devices.
