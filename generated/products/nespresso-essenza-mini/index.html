Our Review

The Nespresso Essenza Mini is for people who want espresso without any effort or learning curve. Insert a pod, press a button, get consistent espresso in 30 seconds. The 19-bar pump extracts properly from Nespresso's aluminum capsules, and the results are remarkably consistent — every shot tastes the same. The machine is tiny (barely bigger than a water bottle) and heats up in 25 seconds. Nespresso offers 30+ capsule varieties from intense ristretto to mild lungo. At $150 (often bundled with a milk frother), it's the easiest path to real espresso. Food Network's 'Best Capsule' pick.
