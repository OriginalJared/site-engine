Our Review

The Weber Q 2200 is the premium portable gas grill for people who want propane convenience in a compact package. Its single burner delivers 12,000 BTU across 280 sq in of porcelain-enameled cast-iron cooking grates — enough for 8 burgers. The push-button ignition, built-in thermometer, and infinite burner control make it dead simple to use. Compact enough for apartment balconies yet capable enough for tailgates and camping trips, it's the go-anywhere gas grill.
