The Withings ScanWatch 2 solves the biggest problem with smartwatches: they look like tech gadgets, not watches. The ScanWatch looks like a $500 analog timepiece — sapphire crystal, stainless steel case, real watch hands — but underneath it packs FDA-cleared ECG, blood oxygen monitoring, respiratory rate tracking, and sleep apnea detection. The small OLED display shows notifications and health data without ruining the classic aesthetic. 30-day battery life means you charge it once a month. For professionals who can't wear an Apple Watch to a boardroom, the ScanWatch is the answer.
