Our Review

The Hisense PX2-Pro is a TV replacement disguised as a projector. Its ultra-short-throw laser engine projects a 130" 4K image from just inches away from the wall — no ceiling mount or dark room required. TriChroma laser technology produces 107% BT.2020 color coverage for cinema-grade color accuracy, and 2,400 lumens of brightness means it works in daylight. Dolby Vision, IMAX Enhanced, and Dolby Atmos decoding make it a complete home cinema system.
