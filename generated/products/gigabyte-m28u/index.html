Our Review

The Gigabyte M28U packs 4K gaming essentials into an affordable package. Its 28" SS IPS panel hits 144Hz with HDMI 2.1 for console gaming and 1ms response time for competitive play. The built-in KVM switch lets you control two PCs with one keyboard and mouse. At $649, it significantly undercuts LG and Samsung while matching their core specs. It's the smart choice for value-conscious 4K gamers.

✅ What We Like

  • Great 4K 144Hz performance for the price
  • KVM switch is a rare and useful feature
  • HDMI 2.1 works great with PS5/Xbox
  • Solid color accuracy out of the box

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • HDR support is minimal (HDR400)
  • Stand is basic — no swivel
  • Speakers are poor quality

Key Features

  • 28" 4K UHD (3840x2160)
