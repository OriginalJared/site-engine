Our Review

The Breville Precision Brewer gives coffee enthusiasts full control over every variable in drip brewing. Six brew modes — Gold, Fast, Strong, Iced, Cold Brew, and My Brew (fully custom) — cover every style imaginable. SCA-certified Gold Cup brewing ensures optimal water temperature and contact time, and the flat-bottom brew basket with adjustable flow rate lets you fine-tune extraction. A thermal carafe keeps coffee hot without burning, and the 60oz capacity handles large households.
