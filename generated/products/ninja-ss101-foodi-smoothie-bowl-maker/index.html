Our Review

The Ninja Foodi SS101 is designed specifically for smoothie bowls, thick blends, and frozen desserts that regular blenders can't handle. Its Ninja Thick-Spread Technology and unique paddle attachment create thick, spoonable textures without over-blending into liquid. Four programs (Smoothie Bowl, Spread, Extract, Smoothie) and a 14oz single-serve cup handle both thick and thin blends. If smoothie bowls are your thing, this is the only blender designed to make them perfectly.
