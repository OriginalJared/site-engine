Our Review

The TP-Link Deco X55 delivers rock-solid mesh Wi-Fi 6 at a price that's hard to argue with. The 3-pack covers up to 6,500 sq ft with dual-band AX3000 speeds, and each node has 3 Gigabit Ethernet ports for wired devices. AI-Driven Mesh learns your network habits and optimizes routing, while HomeShield provides basic network security. For homes that don't need Wi-Fi 6E, this is the sweet spot of price and performance.

✅ What We Like

  • Unbeatable value under $200 for 3-pack mesh
  • 6,500 sq ft coverage eliminates dead zones
  • 3 Ethernet ports per node is generous
  • Deco app is beginner-friendly

⚠️ Worth Noting
