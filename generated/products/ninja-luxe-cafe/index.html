Our Review

The Ninja Luxe Café isn't just an espresso machine — it's an entire coffee system. It makes espresso with a 20-bar pump, brews drip coffee, creates cold brew concentrate, and froths milk with an automatic frother — all in one machine. The built-in grinder handles everything from fine espresso to coarse cold brew. The touchscreen interface guides you through each drink type. CNET's 'Best for Espresso and Drip' pick. At $600, it replaces 3-4 separate devices. If your household has different coffee preferences (espresso for you, drip for your partner, cold brew for summer), the Luxe Café covers everyone.
