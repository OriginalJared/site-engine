Our Review

The LEVOIT Core 300 has been Amazon's #1 best-selling air purifier for years, and for good reason. Its true H13 HEPA filter captures 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns — dust, pollen, pet dander, smoke, and mold spores. The 360-degree air intake pulls air from all sides, and the three fan speeds plus sleep mode cover everything from quick purge to whisper-quiet overnight operation at just 24 dB. It handles rooms up to 1,095 sq ft per hour. At $100, it's the safest recommendation for anyone who wants cleaner air without overthinking it.
