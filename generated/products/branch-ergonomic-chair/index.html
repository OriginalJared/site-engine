Our Review

Branch disrupted the office furniture market by selling direct-to-consumer, cutting out the retail markup that inflates chair prices. The result is a chair with 7 points of adjustment (height, depth, tilt, tension, lumbar, armrest height, armrest width), breathable Italian mesh, and a 7-year warranty — all for $399. The design is clean and minimal, fitting any office aesthetic. It's not as refined as a $1,500 Herman Miller, but for the vast majority of desk workers, it provides 85% of the ergonomic benefit at 25% of the cost. Branch also offers a 30-day free trial.

✅ What We Like

  • Best value ergonomic chair under $500
  • 7-year warranty rivals premium brands
  • Clean, office-appropriate design
  • 30-day risk-free trial

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • Lumbar support isn't as adjustable as premium chairs
  • Seat cushion can flatten after 2-3 years

  • Limited color/fabric options
