Our Review

The XGIMI Horizon Ultra uses a groundbreaking dual-light system combining LED and laser for the best of both worlds — laser brightness with LED color accuracy. Intelligent Screen Adaption automatically adjusts keystone, focus, and screen alignment every time you turn it on — zero manual setup. The Harman Kardon speakers are surprisingly good, and the XGIMI OS based on Android TV provides seamless streaming. Dolby Vision support and 2,300 lumens handle HDR content beautifully.

✅ What We Like

  • Auto setup is truly hands-free — incredible
  • Dual-light tech delivers excellent color + brightness
  • Harman Kardon speakers eliminate need for soundbar
  • Dolby Vision support is rare in projectors

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $1,699 is mid-premium pricing
  • Fan noise is present at full brightness
  • XGIMI OS has fewer apps than Google TV
  • No lens shift — relies on digital correction
