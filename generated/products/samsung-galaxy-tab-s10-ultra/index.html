Samsung Galaxy Tab S10 Ultra

The best Android tablet — 14.6-inch Super AMOLED, S Pen included, and Samsung DeX turns it into a desktop.

★ 4.7 / 5 $1000
Check Price on Amazon Compare All Tablets

Our Review

The Galaxy Tab S10 Ultra is Samsung's everything tablet. The 14.6-inch Super AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate is the largest and most beautiful tablet screen available. The S Pen is included (Apple charges extra), and Samsung's DeX mode transforms the tablet into a full desktop experience with resizable windows. The Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 handles any Android app or game without hesitation. At $1,000 for the base model, it's expensive, but you're getting a tablet that genuinely replaces a laptop for most tasks. The dual front cameras make it the best tablet for video calls.

✅ What We Like

  • Largest, most beautiful tablet display
  • S Pen included — not an extra purchase
  • DeX mode is a genuine productivity tool
  • Best Android tablet, period

⚠️ Worth Noting

  • $1,000+ is premium pricing
  • 14.6" is too big for one-handed use
  • Samsung keyboard cover sold separately
  • Some apps aren't optimized for tablet
