Our Review

The Napoleon Prestige 500 is what happens when you apply premium engineering to a gas grill. Four main burners plus an infrared rear rotisserie burner and an infrared sear station deliver 80,000+ BTU across 900 sq in of total cooking space. Stainless steel construction, iconic WAVE reversible cast-iron grids, and the JETFIRE ignition system ensure restaurant-quality results. It's the grill for serious cooks who want the best and don't mind paying for it.
