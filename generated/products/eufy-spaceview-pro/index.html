Our Review

The eufy SpaceView Pro is for parents who want reliable video monitoring without connecting to the internet. Its encrypted FHSS signal streams directly to a dedicated 5" 720p display — no Wi-Fi, no app, no hacking risk. The wide-angle lens with remote pan, tilt, and zoom covers the whole room, and the 460-foot range handles large homes. Night vision is sharp, two-way audio lets you soothe remotely, and the dedicated monitor means no phone battery drain.
