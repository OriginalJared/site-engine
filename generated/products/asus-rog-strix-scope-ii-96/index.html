Our Review

ASUS' ROG Strix Scope II 96 is built for competitive gamers who want wireless freedom. The ROG NX Snow switches are fast and linear with a short 1.8mm actuation point, and the 96% layout keeps the numpad while freeing up mouse space. Tri-mode connectivity (2.4GHz at 1000Hz, Bluetooth, USB-C) covers every scenario, and the 2.4GHz mode delivers the low latency competitive gamers demand. Per-key RGB, hot-swappable switches, and ROG Armoury Crate software for full customization. The aluminum frame and PBT keycaps add durability. At $180, it's a complete competitive gaming keyboard.
