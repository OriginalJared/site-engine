Our Review

The Philips 5500 LatteGo's killer feature is cleaning simplicity. The LatteGo milk system has just 2 parts that rinse under the faucet in 15 seconds — competitors require disassembling 8+ components. The machine brews 20 hot and iced drinks with one touch, and the ceramic burr grinder preserves bean flavor better than steel grinders. The intuitive touchscreen makes drink selection easy, and the SilentBrew technology reduces noise significantly. At $900, it slots between the budget CASABREWS and the premium Rivelia — offering fully automatic convenience with the easiest maintenance.
