Contact Us

We’d love to hear from you.

Have a question about one of our reviews? Spotted something that needs correcting? Think there’s a product we should be testing? Our editorial team reads every message.

Email

The best way to reach us is by email at hello@halcottmagazine.com. We read every email, though we can’t always respond to each one individually.

What to Reach Out About

  • Corrections. If you believe a specification, price, or detail in a review is inaccurate, let us know and we’ll investigate and update it.
  • Product suggestions. Tell us about a product you’d like one of our specialists to evaluate.
  • General questions. Questions about how we test, rank, or fund our work are always welcome.

A Note on Reviews

We do not accept payment for reviews or for guaranteed placement in our rankings, and we do not accept free products in exchange for favorable coverage. Our editorial team evaluates every product independently. For more on how we work, see our Editorial Policy.

Press & Partnerships

For press inquiries or partnership questions, email us at the address above with “Press” or “Partnership” in the subject line.
