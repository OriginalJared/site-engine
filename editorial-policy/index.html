Editorial Policy

Transparency isn’t optional. Here’s exactly how we review products, how we make money, and what we promise to our readers.

How We Review Products

Every product featured on Halcott Magazine is evaluated by a specialist editor with direct experience in that product category. Our review process follows a consistent methodology:

  • Research: We analyze manufacturer specifications, read existing owner reviews, and identify the most important performance criteria for each product category.
  • Evaluation: Products are assessed on real-world performance, build quality, value for money, ease of use, and long-term reliability.
  • Comparison: We evaluate products against their direct competitors at similar price points.
  • Honest reporting: Every review includes both pros and cons. If a product has a significant weakness, we disclose it.

How We Make Money

Halcott Magazine earns revenue through affiliate commissions. When you click a product link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer.

  • You pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer.
  • We participate in the Amazon Associates Program and may participate in other affiliate programs in the future.
  • Affiliate relationships never influence our product rankings, ratings, or recommendations.

What We Don’t Do

  • We don’t accept payment for reviews. No manufacturer or retailer can pay to be featured or to receive a higher ranking.
  • We don’t accept free products in exchange for coverage.
  • We don’t use sponsored or partnered reviews. Every review reflects the genuine assessment of our editorial team.
  • We don’t guarantee placement. Products earn their ranking based on merit alone.

Corrections and Updates

Product information changes over time. We make every effort to keep our content accurate and current. If you spot an error, please email us at hello@halcottmagazine.com.

Questions About This Policy?

We believe in full transparency. If you have questions about how we review products or how we earn revenue, don’t hesitate to reach out.

Email us at hello@halcottmagazine.com
