Editorial Policy

Transparency isn’t optional. Here’s exactly how we review products, how we make money, and what we promise to our readers.

How We Review Products

Every product featured on Halcott Magazine is evaluated by a specialist editor with direct experience in that product category. Our review process follows a consistent methodology across all categories:

  • Research: We analyze manufacturer specifications, read existing owner reviews, and identify the most important performance criteria for each product category.
  • Evaluation: Products are assessed on real-world performance, build quality, value for money, ease of use, and long-term reliability. We weigh these factors based on what actually matters to buyers — not what looks good on a spec sheet.
  • Comparison: We evaluate products against their direct competitors at similar price points. A budget product is judged against other budget products, not against a flagship twice its price.
  • Honest reporting: Every review includes both pros and cons. If a product has a significant weakness, we disclose it — even if it’s otherwise excellent. We believe you deserve the full picture before spending your money.

Our ratings reflect the overall value a product delivers relative to its price and competition. A 4.5-rated budget product isn’t “worse” than a 4.7-rated premium product — it’s excellent for what it is.

How We Make Money

Halcott Magazine earns revenue through affiliate commissions. When you click a product link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer. This is the standard business model used by most independent review publications.

Here’s what this means in practice:

  • You pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer. Our commission comes from the retailer, not from you.
  • We participate in the Amazon Associates Program and may participate in other affiliate programs in the future.
  • Affiliate relationships never influence our product rankings, ratings, or recommendations. Our editorial team operates independently from our revenue operations.

We chose this model specifically because it aligns our interests with yours: we only succeed when we recommend products that you’re genuinely happy with.

What We Don’t Do

  • We don’t accept payment for reviews. No manufacturer or retailer can pay to be featured or to receive a higher ranking.
  • We don’t accept free products in exchange for coverage. Accepting free products creates an implicit obligation that compromises objectivity.
  • We don’t use “sponsored” or “partnered” reviews. Every review on this site reflects the genuine assessment of our editorial team.
  • We don’t guarantee placement. Products earn their ranking based on merit alone.

How We Select Products

We aim to cover the products that matter most to real buyers. Our selection process considers:

  • Market relevance: We prioritize products that are widely available, currently sold by major retailers, and actively purchased by consumers.
  • Category coverage: Within each category, we include a range of price points — from budget-friendly options to premium picks — so every reader can find something that fits their needs and budget.
  • Reader demand: We pay attention to what our readers are searching for and expand our coverage to match real consumer interest.

Corrections and Updates

Product information changes over time. Prices fluctuate, models get updated, and products are occasionally discontinued. We make every effort to keep our content accurate and current.

If you spot an error or believe a review needs updating, please email us at hello@halcottmagazine.com. We take accuracy seriously and will investigate promptly.

Questions About This Policy?

We believe in full transparency. If you have questions about how we review products, how we earn revenue, or anything else about our editorial process, don’t hesitate to reach out.
