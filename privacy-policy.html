Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Halcott Magazine (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit our website. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Specifically:

  • Information you provide. If you email us, we receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to you.
  • Automatically collected information. Like most websites, we and our analytics providers may collect standard technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate location, referring page, and the pages you view. This helps us understand what content is useful and improve the site.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site and to measure traffic. We may use third-party analytics services for this purpose. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some parts of the site may work differently if you do.

Affiliate Links

Halcott Magazine participates in affiliate advertising programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the retailer may set cookies and may pay us a commission at no additional cost to you. These programs may collect information about your visit in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any retailer you visit through our links.

How We Use Information

We use the information described above to operate and improve the website, respond to inquiries, measure the performance of our content, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party providers for hosting, analytics, and affiliate tracking. These providers process data on our behalf or under their own policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites you reach through our links.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us using the details below. You can also control cookies through your browser and opt out of many analytics and advertising cookies through your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

This site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, email us at hello@halcottmagazine.com or visit our Contact page.
